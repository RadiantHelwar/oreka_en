# Eiren

![Eiren.png|400](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Eiren.png)

**Titles**: The White Lady, The Bird of Peace, The White Light
**Symbol**: An olive branch
**Associated Number**: 1
**Domains**: Peace, Conciliation
**Description**: Eiren is commonly depicted as a young lady, long black hair blowing in the wind, and dressed in a simple white dress. Many depictions present her in front of or under an olive tree and next to doves.
