# Oreka Pantheon

The [[Lore/Pantheons/Church of Aureon\|Church of Aureon]] teaches that the deities existed before the [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] and who by a covenant with him withdrew to the heavenly kingdom, setting him up as their **Divine Spokesman**.

These Deities could not act on the material plane since their positions were always counteracted by their opposite. With [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] as his Spokesman, his power could at last be applied to Oreka's welfare.

Today they are revered as aspects of Aureon himself.

Their symbol is a chariot wheel, each spoke topped with an abstract symbol, representing each of the Gods, and the Symbol of Aureon in the centre.
