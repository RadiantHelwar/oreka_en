# Chara

! [350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Chara.png)

**Titles**: La Risueña, The Lady of the Links, The Lady of Wine
**Symbol**: Overflowing Chalice
**Associated Number**: V
**Domains**: Joy, Celebration, Love, Freedom
**Description**: A lady of beautiful figure, dressed in a red and purple tunic, always with a cup in her hand overflowing with wine. She is pictured smiling, and surrounded by couples and celebrants.
**Cult**:
