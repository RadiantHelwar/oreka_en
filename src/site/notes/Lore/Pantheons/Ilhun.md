# Ilhun

![Ilhun.png|400](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Ilhun.png)

**Titles**: The Night, The Subterfuge, The Silence
**Symbol**: Open triangle inserted in a circle
**Associated Number**: IX
**Domains**: Darkness, Secrets, Oblivion
**Description**: A man, face covered with a pointed mask, crouched in the shadows, or standing alone on a pedestal, arms crossed or with a finger to his lips.
