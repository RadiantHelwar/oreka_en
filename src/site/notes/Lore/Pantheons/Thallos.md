# Thallos

! [226](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Thallos.png)

**Titles**: The Sprout, The Healer, the Wise Granny
**Symbol**: A sprout with three leaves
**Associated Number**: III
**Domains**: Life, fertility, abundance, physical healing
**Description**: An elderly woman leaning on a branch full of leaves, dressed in a cloak covering her form, with thousands of flowers at her feet, on her shoulders and behind her back. On many occasions her representations also include a newborn child in her arms.
