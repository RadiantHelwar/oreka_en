# Aureon

![Aureon.png|400](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Aureon.png)

**Titles**: The Father, The Saviour, The Golden One
**Symbol**: A double-bladed sword on a perfect golden circle.
**Associated Number**: ∞
**Domains**: Order, Faith, Golden Light
**Description**: A young human in golden armour, with curly chestnut hair. A hole in the centre of his chest shows a distinct galaxy, as do his eyes. He always carries his double-bladed sword: [[Argia\|Argia]] and a perfect circle of gold behind it, denoting it as the centre of the Pantheon.
