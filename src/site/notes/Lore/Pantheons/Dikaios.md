# Dikaios

![Dikaios.png|250](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Dikaios.png)

**Titles**: The Blind Man, The Sword of Punishment, The Unbreakable Bow
**Symbol**: Star/meteorite ascending
**Associated Number**: IV
**Domains**: Honour, Promises, Vows, Oaths, Justice
**Description**: A man of tall, slender figure, covered in a dark robe and gleaming ceremonial armor, which always includes a half helmet that covers his face completely, and a ribbon weaves from wrist to wrist across his shoulders. He always carries a bejewelled, straight sword of colossal size.
