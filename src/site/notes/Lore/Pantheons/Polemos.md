# Polemos

! [250](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Polemos.png)

**Titles**: The Ancient Tradition, The Conflict, River of Blood
**Symbol**: Mangual
**Associated Number**: X
**Domains**: War, Conquest, Conflict and Conflict Resolution
**Description**: A woman covered in armour and carrying a mangual, never wielding it in combat. She is always depicted as calm and collected, surrounded by people in conflict, sometimes standing between the two sides. She is sometimes depicted as supporting one side or the other, but these depictions are always conflicting.
