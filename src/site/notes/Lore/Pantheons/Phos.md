# Phos

! [400](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Dioses/Phos.png)

**Titles**: Morning Star, The Radiant One, Veritasi
**Symbol**: Eight-pointed Star
**Associated Number**: II
**Domains**: Light, Truth, Revelation
**Description**: Phos is commonly pictured as a short-bearded man floating with open arms and four rays of light behind him, imitating his symbol. He wears an orange robe and a golden eight-pointed crown as his only adornment.
