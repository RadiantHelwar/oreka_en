# Ancestral Lineages

In Oreka, several lineages exist: groups of people linked by bloodline, tradition, culture, or ritual, who possess extraordinary supernatural powers.

- [[Lore/Aitherion\|Aitherion]] – Heirs to the royal house of Haizea. Their soul manifests externally as a [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]] or Pneuma, a living creature—usually a bird—that embodies the essence of the Aitherion, with phenomenal powers that vary between individuals. _(Not playable)_
- [[Chrysos\|Chrysos]] – Claiming descent from Aureon in his mortal days and **Roz**, his wife and mistress of the shadows. They are always considered Aasimar, and their powers encompass the duality of light and shadow. _(Playable)_
- [[Ironwilled\|Ironwilled]] – Orcs, half-orcs, and occasionally humans, possessing a latent ability to transform part of their body into fine metallic particles, which can be condensed to harden or scattered to soften. This grants them temporary resistance to damage or advantage on certain saving throws or physical challenges. _(Playable)_
- [[Sendatu\|Sendatu]] – Commonly goliath or human, but always fire genasi. Their powers allow them to both heal and harm, manipulating the forces of fire and vitality. _(Playable)_
