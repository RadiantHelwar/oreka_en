# Ancestral Lineages

In Oreka there are different lineages, of people linked by bloodlines, tradition, culture or rituals, who enjoy overwhelming supernatural powers.

- [[Lore/Aitherion\|Aitherion]] heirs to the royal house of Haizea, their soul exists on the outside as a [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]] or Pneuma, a living creature, usually a bird that embodies the soul of the Aitherion, with phenomenal powers depending on the individual. (not playable)_
- [[Chrysos\|Chrysos]] Aureon, claiming descent from Aureon when he was still human, and **Roz**, his wife and mistress of the shadows. Their powers encompass the duality of light and shadow _(playable)_.
- The [[Férreos\|Férreos]] are Orcs, Half-orcs and sometimes Humans with a latent ability to turn part of their body into fine metallic particles, condensing them to harden or scattering them to soften. (Basically they can gain damage resistance to 1 attack or gain advantage on saves/PB times) *(playable)*.
- [[Sendatu\|Sendatu]] They are commonly goliath or human, but always genasi of fire. Their powers allow them to restore others and destroy others. *(playable)
