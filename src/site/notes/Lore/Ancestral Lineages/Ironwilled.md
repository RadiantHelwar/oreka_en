# Ironwilled

Descendants from Geran the Indomitable. 



## Origin Feat

### Ironwilled

**Requirements:** Orc, or rarely orc-blooded members of other species

Your body carries a latent metallic essence, allowing it to harden or disperse in moments of danger.

You gain the following benefits.

**Metallic Resilience.** You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

You can use this feature in one of the following ways:

- **Harden.** When you take damage, you can use your Reaction to gain Resistance to that instance of damage.
- **Disperse.** When you make a Strength, Dexterity, or Constitution saving throw, you can gain Advantage on that roll.
