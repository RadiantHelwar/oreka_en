# Ancestral Lineages

In Oreka, several lineages exist: groups of people linked by bloodline, tradition, culture, or ritual, who possess extraordinary supernatural powers.

- [[Lore/Ancestral Lineages/Aitherion\|Aitherion]] – Heirs to the royal house of Haizea. Their soul manifests externally as a [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]] or Pneuma, a living creature—usually a bird—that embodies the essence of the Aitherion, with phenomenal powers that vary between individuals. _(Not playable)_
- [[Lore/Ancestral Lineages/Chrysos\|Chrysos]] – Aasimar of human ascendancy, claiming descent from Aureon in his mortal days and **Roz**, his wife and mistress of the shadows. Their powers encompass the duality of light and shadow. _(Playable)_
- [[Lore/Ancestral Lineages/Ironwilled\|Ironwilled]] – Orcs and individuals of Orc ascendancy, possessing a latent ability to transform part of their body into fine metallic particles, which can be condensed to harden or scattered to soften. _(Playable)_
- [[Lore/Ancestral Lineages/Sendatu\|Sendatu]] – Fire genasi of Goliath or Human ascendancy. Their powers allow them to both heal and harm, manipulating the forces of fire and vitality. _(Playable)_
- [[Lore/Ancestral Lineages/Echo-Born\|Echo-Born]], they are dwarves and some dwarven-blooded members of other species. They are known as descendants of the dwarven Primarchs, and their power lies in their voice, capable of shaping minds and the world. *(Playable)*
