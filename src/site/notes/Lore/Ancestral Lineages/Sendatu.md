# Sendatu

Descendants of Mill of Infinite Force, and Amitera of the Steady Hand. (more text will be here at some point)


## Origin Feat

### Sendatu

**Requirements**: Fire Genasi of Goliath or Human descent

You carry the flame of Mill, a primordial fire that both destroys and restores.

You gain the following benefits.

**Cleansing Flame.** You can use this feature a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

As an Action, choose a creature you can see within 30 feet of yourself. Roll 1d8 and add your Proficiency Bonus, then apply the result as follows:

- If the target is an ally, it regains Hit Points equal to the roll.
- If the target is an enemy, it takes Fire damage equal to the roll.

**Flame Duality.** When you use Cleansing Flame, you can choose a second creature within 10 feet of the first target. The second creature is affected in the opposite way (healing if the first took damage, or damage if the first was healed).

**Piercing Flame.** Fire damage dealt by this feat ignores Resistance to Fire damage.
