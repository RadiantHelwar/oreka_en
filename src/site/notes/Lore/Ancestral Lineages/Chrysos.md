# Chrysos

Ancenstral lineage of descendants of Aureon and Roz. (more text will be here)


## Origin Feat

### Chrysos

**Requirements**: Aasimar (of human or half-human ancestry)

You descend from Aureon, lord of golden light, and Roz, lady of shadows. Their duality lives within you.

You gain the following benefits.

**Dual Aspect.** When you finish a Long Rest, choose one of the following aspects:

- **Radiant Aspect.** Once per turn when you hit a creature with an attack or deal damage to a creature with a spell, you can deal extra Radiant damage equal to your Proficiency Bonus. The target sheds dim light in a 10-foot radius and can’t benefit from being Invisible until the start of your next turn.
- **Umbral Aspect.** As a Bonus Action, you can teleport up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space you can see that is in Dim Light or Darkness. You can use this Bonus Action a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest. In addition, you have Advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks while in Dim Light or Darkness.

**Inner Duality.** Once per Long Rest, as a Bonus Action, you can switch from your current aspect to the other. When you use **Inner Duality**, you can immediately use one of the benefits of your new aspect without expending a use.
