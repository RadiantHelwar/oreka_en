# Tisaran Glass

The so-called _Tisaran Glass_, known outside Zharak territory as _Prismatic Glass_, is a unique material in Oreka. Transparent, almost invisible under certain lights, it has an extraordinarily light structure, comparable to an almost empty substance. Despite this, its strength exceeds that of the most refined steel.

It cannot be forged by conventional heat. On the contrary: it begins to become malleable at temperatures approaching -100°C, provided a constant flow of magical energy is maintained through it. Under these conditions it can be shaped. Once solidified, it requires much greater extreme cold or a magical shock of enormous intensity to regain that pliable state. Under normal circumstances, it is more feasible to chip it than to reform it, though breaking it is still a considerable feat, except when using tools made from the glass itself.

Its near-empty structure allows magic to flow with exceptional ease within it. It is simple to enchant, stores energy for long periods, and strengthens its integrity when channelling arcane power. It is therefore coveted as a focus, ritual component and weapon. However, the [[Regions/Glastra/Tisaran Theocracy\|Tisaran Theocracy]] strictly regulates their use. Most of the fragments remain in temples or sacred sites, and only limited quantities leave the territory under very specific agreements.
