# Tisaran Glass

The so-called _Tisaran Glass_, known outside Zharak territory as _Prismatic Glass_, is a unique material in Oreka. Transparent and almost invisible under certain lighting, it possesses an extraordinarily light structure, akin to an almost empty substance. Despite this, its strength exceeds that of the finest steel.

It cannot be forged by conventional heat. On the contrary, it becomes malleable at temperatures approaching -100 °C, provided a constant flow of magical energy is maintained through it. Under these conditions, it can be shaped. Once solidified, it requires either far greater extremes of cold or a magical shock of tremendous intensity to return it to a pliable state. Under ordinary circumstances, it is easier to chip than to reform, though breaking it remains a considerable feat—except when using tools crafted from the glass itself.

Its near-empty structure allows magic to flow with exceptional ease. It is readily enchanted, stores energy over long periods, and its integrity strengthens when channelling arcane power. As a result, it is highly prized as a focus, ritual component, and weapon. However, the [[Regions/Glastra/Tisaran Theocracy\|Tisaran Theocracy]] strictly regulates its use. Most fragments remain in temples or sacred sites, and only limited quantities leave the territory under carefully controlled agreements.
