# Tercentenary of Victory

**Category:** Celebration
**Date:** Trina 17, 7307, DE

Every year, Oreka sings the jubilation of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Receso\|Receso]] the festival commemorating the victory of the [[Escudo de la Luz\|Escudo de la Luz]] and its heroine, [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] and his adventurers, over the forces of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Señor Oscuro Menras]]. But this year, three centuries later, the celebration reaches a milestone: the **Tricentenary of the Victory**. Never before has the memory of that deed received such a grandiose tribute.

Every city in the world prepares for the occasion, to a greater or lesser extent; however, Riza, the star city of Lakua, dresses up for the occasion with particular relish. Streets, squares and temples are decked out in the golden light of the [[Regions/Glastra/Ilios\|Gran Árbol]] while the [[Lore/Pantheons/Church of Aureon\|Church of Aureon]] decrees that the capital will be the epicentre of all festivities. Renowned merchants, glory-seeking adventurers, dignitaries from distant lands and curious onlookers from all over Oreka descend on the city, eager not to miss a moment of the festivities.

Visitors will find everything from jousting tournaments and gladiatorial combat to plays, street performances, fairground games and raffles promising extraordinary prizes. Rumours circulate in the air that no one dares to hush up: it is said that a fireworks display will light up the sky like never before, capable of eclipsing even the light of [[Regions/Glastra/Ilios\|Ilios]] and take the breath away from all who behold it.
