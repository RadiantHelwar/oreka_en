# Demons of Menras

The four demonic generals, under the command of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] during the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]]. Each of them ruled from one of the damned four [[Lore/Ancient Past/Faros de Oreka\|Faros de Oreka]] and commanded one of the various armies of darkness:

| Demon | Army |
| ------------------------------------ | --------------------------- |
| [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] Menras | [[Los Oscuros\|Los Oscuros]] |
| [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]] | [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Inmortal Army\|Inmortal Army]] |
| [[Oneiros, Señor de la Pesadilla\|Oneiros, Señor de la Pesadilla]] | [[Huestes de la Pesadilla\|Huestes de la Pesadilla]] |
| [[Drya, Corazón del Enjambre\|Drya, Corazón del Enjambre]] | [[Enjambre Infinito\|Enjambre Infinito]] |
| [[Astrikos, el Ascua Imperecedera\|Astrikos, el Ascua Imperecedera]] | [[Armada Estelar\|Armada Estelar]] |
Of these, only [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]] remains active, trapped in the north behind the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]] thanks to the [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]] and the troops of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Northwind\|Northwind]].
