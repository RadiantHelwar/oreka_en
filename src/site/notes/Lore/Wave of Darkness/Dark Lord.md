## Ancient Dark Lord

Menras started the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]] **354 years ago**, unleashing a period of unprecedented terror and devastation in Oreka.

Its origins lie in the [[Lore/Cosmology/The Rot\|The Rot]] and in the initial corruption of the [[Lore/Ancient Past/Four Fiend Kings\|Four Fiend Kings]]. When [[Regions/Glastra/Ilios\|Ilios]] was infected by this influence, corruption eventually emerged in what is known as the Rot. To contain it, Aureon removed the battered tree and replaced it with the Golden Light construct that shines over Oreka today.

Menras emerged from the remains of the corrupted Ilios, taking humanoid form. His body was composed of divine wood, but his will was dominated by abyssal corruption. His purpose was diametrically opposed to that of Ilios: to spread the darkness of the Abyss over Oreka. His first act was to eclipse the Ilios of Golden Light, blotting it from the skies and plunging the populace into despair.

For more than five decades, his influence spread across much of the world, and armies under his command spread fear, destruction and famine. In the last six years alone, with the advent of heroin [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] and their group of adventurers blessed by [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] The tide of destruction began to recede, until Menras was finally corralled in the [[Faro Skia\|Faro Skia]] near the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]. His defeat was recorded in the year **7007 DE**, and the remains of his [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Inmortal Army\|Inmortal Army]] remain locked behind the glacier, under the custody of the [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]] together with [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]].
