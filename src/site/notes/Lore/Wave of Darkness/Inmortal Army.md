# Immortal Army

! [300](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Monstruos/sombrainmortal.jpg)

The **Immortal Army**, commanded by [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]] is made up of extra-planar creatures originating from the [[Lore/Cosmology/Shadow Plane\|Shadow Plane]]. They come in many forms and hierarchies, but all share a common nature.

They are virtually invisible: in many instances, only their red eyes can be distinguished in the gloom. Yet their shadows are sharp, complex and precise, as if their real bodies were these dark projections. They move and exist in the same unnatural way as shadows: under unstable lights they twist and slide, growing or shrinking depending on their proximity to the light source.

As a result, those who encounter them soon learn to watch their own shadow, for when the enemy's shadows interact with it...
