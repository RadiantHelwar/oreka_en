# Menras' Demons

The four demonic generals, under the command of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] during the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]]. Each of them ruled from one of the damned four [[Lore/Ancient Past/Lighthouses of Oreka\|Lighthouses of Oreka]] and commanded one of the various armies of darkness:

| Demon                                | Army                |
| ------------------------------------ | ------------------- |
| [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] Menras                 | [[The Dark Ones\|The Dark Ones]]   |
| [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]]    | [[Immortal Army\|Immortal Army]]   |
| [[Oneiros, Lord of Nightmares\|Oneiros, Lord of Nightmares]]      | [[Nightmare Hosts\|Nightmare Hosts]] |
| [[Drya, Heart of the Swarm\|Drya, Heart of the Swarm]]         | [[Infinite Swarm\|Infinite Swarm]]  |
| [[Astrikos, the Imperishable Ember\|Astrikos, the Imperishable Ember]] | [[Stellar Armada\|Stellar Armada]]  |
Of these, only [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]] remains active, trapped in the north behind the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]] thanks to the [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]] and the troops of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Northwind\|Northwind]].
