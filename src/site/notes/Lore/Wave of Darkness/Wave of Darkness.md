# Wave of Darkness

> **Event:** War
> **Start:** 5th of Defta of 6953, DE
> End:** Trina 17, 7007, DE

Name by which the [[Lore/Pantheons/Church of Aureon\|Church of Aureon]] knows the war led by the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] to dominate Oreka and bring it under the control of darkness.

On 5 Defta 6953 DE, the [[Hierofante\|Hierofante]] [[Erldatum IV\|Erldatum IV]] officially declared the **Demon Menras** to be the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] after he went to [[Regions/Glastra/Ieró\|Ieró]] to threaten them directly.

>Erldatum IV's speech on the balcony of the Sacred Palace
>People of Iero.
>Sons and daughters of the Light.
>Oreka whole who listens.
>
>Today I do not speak to reassure you.
>I speak because the Darkness has knocked at our doors... and has been rejected.
>
>In this very Palace, beneath these consecrated stones, an unholy presence dared to manifest itself.
>
>Not as a man. Not as a messenger. But as a threat.
>
>He who calls himself **Menras** came uninvited, his shadow heavy as the abyss. He came to challenge the order of the world. He came to reclaim what Aureon sealed with fire and sacrifice.
>
>I tell you plainly:
> >The **Dark Lord** has shown his face. He summoned the *Beacons*. He spoke the *Seals*. He spoke of the day when they would be opened and of a world "delivered" from the divine will. The weight of the **Four Demon Kings**, sealed by our Father Saviour, was invoked.
>And when his voice tried to break this sacred enclosure, Aureon was with me.
>By the Light of the god, his threat was repulsed. By the faith that binds us, his presence was driven out. But before he fled, he revealed his intentions.
>
>The **Lights of Oreka** are not forgotten relics. They are the locks of hell.
>They are the only thing keeping in chains the **Four Demon Kings**, the devourers of ages, the primordial enemies of Creation.
>
>Therefore, before you and before posterity, I proclaim:
> >- Menras is the Dark Lord.
>
>- Look up now. Where once shone Ilios, the living testimony of the alliance with Aureon, it is gone. It is gone. Do you think it is by chance?
>No.
>It is the mark of his passing. It is the veil of his aura, a shadow so thick that even the Light must break through.
>But hear me well, Oreka's people:
>That the Tree does not shine does not mean that the Light is gone. It means that it is being challenged. Ilios will shine again when the obstacle is destroyed. When the shadow is broken. When the Dark Lord is vanquished.
>And that day will not be brought by a God descending from heaven. You will bring it.
>
>Menras has been rejected today, but he will come back. Not with empty words, but with armies. Not with promises, but with death and corruption. He will bring a **Wave of Darkness** into the world that we must repel with a **Shield of Light** of our creation.
>
>That is why I call you today, not to fear, but to preparation. Aureon did not give us the Light to hide, but to raise it against the night.
>Let the hammers be tempered. Let the walls be strengthened. Let hearts not falter.
>For when the Dark Lord advances, Oreka will answer with one voice.
>
>And when he falls, when his aura dissipates, when the Beacons are sealed...
>- Ilios will shine again.
>- The Light will reclaim the sky.
>- And history will remember that the people of Aureon did not kneel.
>
>- For Aureon. For Oreka. For the Light that will not be extinguished.

After the start of the war, the Dark Lord attacked the [[Lore/Ancient Past/Lighthouses of Oreka\|Lighthouses of Oreka]] He managed to awaken a fraction of the power of the Four Demon Kings despite Oreka's seals. These semi-liberated entities are known as the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Demonios de Menras\|Demonios de Menras]]. Between the Dark Lord and his Demons, they led five great armies:

The Wave of Darkness came to cover much of Oreka. Menras won battle after battle, until the heroine [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] joined the [[Escudo de la Luz\|Escudo de la Luz]]. She and her band of adventurers defeated the Demons of Menras one by one, and after their defeats the troops of the Army of Light were able to retake the beacons.

The Wave of Darkness ended on the 17th of Trina 7007 DE, when Sera headed for the Payment Glacier to destroy the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]]. In her place on the battlefield was Menras himself, and after a vicious battle the heroine emerged victorious, defeating the Dark Lord.

The Wave of Darkness divided the world into two camps. Several countries and kingdoms joined the side of Darkness:

- [[Marquesado de Haizea\|Reino de Haizea]]
- [[Regions/Glastra/Kertutai\|Kertutai]] the Wasp Kingdom
- [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] Empire of the Sun
- The [[Horda de Serra\|Horda de Serra]]
- The savage tribes of [[Díkaii Timoría\|Díkaii Timoría]]
