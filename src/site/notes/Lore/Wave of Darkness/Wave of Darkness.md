# Wave of Darkness

> **Event:** War
> **Start:** 5th of Defta of 6953, GC
> End:** Trina 17, 7007, GC

Name by which the [[Lore/Pantheons/Church of Aureon\|Church of Aureon]] knows the war led by the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] to dominate Oreka and bring it under the control of darkness.

On 5 Defta 6953 DE, the [[Hierophant\|Hierophant]] [[Erldatum IV\|Erldatum IV]] officially declared the **Demon Menras** to be the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] after he went to [[Regions/Glastra/Ieró\|Ieró]] to threaten them directly.

After the start of the war, the Dark Lord attacked the [[Lore/Ancient Past/Lighthouses of Oreka\|Lighthouses of Oreka]] He managed to awaken a fraction of the power of the Four Fiend Kings despite Aureon's seals. These semi-liberated entities are known as the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Menras' Demons\|Menras' Demons]]. Between the Dark Lord and his Demons, they led five great armies:

The Wave of Darkness came to cover much of Oreka. Menras won battle after battle, until the heroine [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] joined the [[Shield of Light\|Shield of Light]]. She and her band of adventurers defeated the Menras' Demons one by one, and after their defeats the troops of the Army of Light were able to retake the beacons.

The Wave of Darkness ended on the 17th of Trina 7007 DE, when Sera headed for the Pagos Glacier to destroy the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]]. In her place on the battlefield was Menras himself, and after a vicious battle the heroine emerged victorious, defeating the Dark Lord.

The Wave of Darkness divided the world into two camps. Several countries and kingdoms joined the side of Darkness:

- The Kingdom (nor Marquisate) of [[Regions/Glastra/Haizea\|Haizea]]]
- [[Regions/Glastra/Kertutai\|Kertutai]] the Wasp Kingdom
- [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]], Empire of the Sun
- The [[Horde of Serra\|Horde of Serra]]
- The savage tribes of [[Díkaii Timoría\|Díkaii Timoría]]
