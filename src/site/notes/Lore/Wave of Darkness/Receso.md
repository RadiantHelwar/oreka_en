# The Recess

**Category:** Celebration
**Date:** 17th of Trina, annually

Date commemorating the victory of the Allies in the [[Escudo de la Luz\|Escudo de la Luz]] under the command of [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] and their adventurers, under the grace of [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] against the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]] and its [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]].

Each society organises the festivities differently, and with varying levels of enthusiasm, but what is certain is that the date is celebrated throughout Oreka, with very few exceptions.
