# Aitherion


## Aitherion Lineage

>[!Quote] Excerpt from the _Book of the Noble Wings_, compiled by the scholar Phanios of Tramesa, year 1042 GC
>
>
>It is not the wind that lifts the Aitherion, but the soul that spreads its wings.

The Aitherion lineage is one of the oldest and most unique in Oreka, born of the union—by blood or by blessing—between the divinity of a **Kipouros** and humanity. Its members are recognisable not only by their noble bearing, but also by the unbreakable bond they maintain with their [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]], outer souls that manifest as winged creatures.

Once kings of Haizea, they now preside over a reduced Marquisate, yet their name still commands respect and a certain veneration. Among them, the ability to manifest a Pneuma is considered the hallmark of their lineage, a symbol of both legitimacy and duty.

## Heraldry

![Aitherion.png|300](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Otros/Heraldicas/Aitherion.png)

The Aitherion coat of arms depicts a deep blue field upon which a golden eagle soars with spread wings amidst silver clouds. The bird carries an arrow in its talons, symbolising speed and will, while a crown floats above its head as a reminder of its royal past.

>[!Quote] Motto
>**"Haizeak garra eramaten du"** ("The Wind Carries the Flame")


## Family structure

For centuries, the lineage has been divided into **two castes**:

- **Main Caste** (_Haize Nagusiak_): direct descendants of the ruling line. They bear the full coat of arms and receive political and martial training from childhood.

- **Secondary Caste** (_Haize Bigarrenak_): Minor branches, born of alliances or collateral lines. They perform diplomatic, military, and territorial administrative duties, generally acting as servants, protectors, or seconds to members of the Main Caste. Members of the Haize Bigarrenak include humans as well as members of other races.    

Although social differences exist between the two castes, their respective [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]] show no significant differences, both sharing the same potential. Rivalry between the castes has shaped much of the Marquisate’s internal history, sometimes through intrigue, sometimes through cooperation.

## Notable ancestors

- **Alkon Aitherion "_The Infinite Wing_"** (6th century GC): Ruled at the pinnacle of the kingdom of Haizea. His Pneuma, _Skýfylax_, was a colossal eagle whose wings could cover the entire palace. Under his shadow, treaties were signed that maintained peace for three generations.

- **Eirene Aitherion "_The Rainsinger_"** (8th century GC): Diplomat and poetess. Her Pneuma, a golden cicada called _Kymaphone_, could alter the weather with her song, bringing rain or clearing the skies as required by the court.

- **Vaelios Aitherion "_The Wind Flamer_"** (10th century GC): General and strategist. His Pneuma, a falcon named _Pyraoros_, combined fire and air currents, capable of setting entire fleets ablaze from the sky.

## Current Presence

Although the main line has lost much of its territorial power, it still maintains political and military influence. Prominent members include a high-ranking representative in the [[Lore/Pantheons/Seven Swords\|Seven Swords]] of Aureon, a testament to the bloodline’s continuing presence among Oreka’s elite.
