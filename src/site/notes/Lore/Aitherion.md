# Aitherion


## Aitherion Lineage

>[!Quote]
>Excerpt from the _Book of the Noble Wings_, compiled by the scholar Phanios of Tramesa, year 1042 DE
>
>It is not the wind that lifts the Aitherion, but the soul that spreads its wings.

The Aitherion lineage is one of the oldest and most unique in Oreka, born of the union - by blood or by blessing - between the divinity of a **Kipouros** and humanity. Its members are recognisable not only by their noble bearing, but also by the unbreakable bond they maintain with their [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]] The outer souls that take the form of a winged creature.

They were once kings of Haizea, and even today, reduced to a Marquisate, their name commands respect and a certain veneration. Among them, the ability to manifest a Pneuma is seen as the hallmark of their lineage, a symbol of their legitimacy and duty.

## Heraldry

! [300](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Otros/Heraldicas/Aitherion.png)

The Aitherion coat of arms shows a deep blue field, on which a golden eagle with spread wings soars amid silver clouds. The bird carries an arrow in its talons, a symbol of speed and will, and a crown floats above its head, a reminder of its royal past.

## Family structure

For centuries, the lineage has been divided into **two castes**:

- **Main Caste** (_Haize Nagusiak_): direct descendants of the ruling line. They bear the full coats of arms and receive political and martial training from childhood.

- Secondary Caste** (_Haize Bigarrenak_): Minor branches, born from alliances or collateral branches. They carry out diplomatic, military and territorial administration tasks. They generally act as servants, protectors or seconds of members of the Main Caste. Members of Haize Bigarrenak include members of races other than human.

Although there are social differences between the two castes, their respective [[Lore/Creatures/Pneumathoi\|Pneumathoi]] The two castes do not have significant differences, both share the same potential. Rivalry between the two castes has marked part of the internal history of the Marquisate, sometimes with intrigue, sometimes with cooperation.

## Notable ancestors

- Alkon Aitherion "The Infinite Wing "** (6th century AD) Ruled at the pinnacle of the kingdom of Haizea. His Pneuma, _Skýfylax_, was a colossal eagle capable of covering the entire palace with his wings. Under his shadow treaties were signed that maintained peace for three generations.

- Eirene Aitherion "The Rainsinger "** (8th century AD) Diplomat and poetess. Her Pneuma, a golden cicada called _Kymaphone_, could alter the weather with her song, bringing rain or clearing the skies as the court required.

- Vaelios Aitherion "The Wind Flamer "** (10th century AD) General and strategist. His Pneuma, a falcon called _Pyraoros_, combined fire and air currents, capable of setting whole fleets ablaze from the sky.

## Current Presence

Although the mainline has reduced its territorial power, it still maintains political and military influence. Prominent members include a high-ranking representative in the [[Lore/Pantheons/Seven Swords\|Seven Swords]] of Aureon, a symbol that the bloodline is still among Oreka's elite.
