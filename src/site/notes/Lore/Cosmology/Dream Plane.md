# Dream Plane

The Dream Plane is one of the Planes of Existence and one of the closest to the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]].

Unlike other planes, its substance is not made up of matter or energy in the conventional sense, but of the very dreams of the conscious creatures of the mundane plane. Every sleeping thought, every memory, every fear or desire that arises during mortal rest leaves an imprint on this plane. Or perhaps it is the other way around, and every fragment of this plane leaves an imprint on the dreams of mortals.

As a result, the Dream Plane is extraordinarily malleable. Distances are subjective, time can be stretched or compressed, and the environment can be completely transformed from one instant to the next. A castle can become a forest, an ocean an endless corridor, and a conversation can last for years or just a breath.

The native inhabitants of this plane are known as **Figments**. These entities play the roles that appear in the dreams of mortals: enemies, allies, guides, monsters, or mere figurants. However, there is debate among scholars as to the true nature of this relationship. It is unclear whether the Figments take on forms in response to mortal dreams, or whether the dreams of mortals are mere glimpses into the lives and actions of the Figments.

The Figments possess multiple possible forms. During their early years of existence they switch between them with ease, but over time most of them eventually adopt one predominant form.

The most common forms are:

### Nightmare Lords
Incarnations of mortal fears and traumas. Their function is to confront dreamers with their past, testing them during their rest. Though feared, many sages believe they serve a necessary function: to temper the spirit of mortals.

### Guardians of Identity
Figments that protect dreamers from the mental deterioration that constant exposure to dream chaos could produce. Their dreams are often comforting, familiar or protective, helping to preserve the emotional stability and identity of the sleeper.

### Night Terrors
Entities that feed on the suffering of mortals. They induce intense and disturbing nightmares, drawing energy from the fear, anguish and despair they provoke.

### Chaos Warlocks
The most common form among the Figments. These entities exist without direct attention to mortals, devoted to their own whims and interests. Their mere presence provokes strange, chaotic or seemingly absurd dreams, which can produce any effect on those who experience them.

The regions of the Dream Plane closest to the Mundane Plane tend to be more coherent and stable, reflecting dreams that are more structured or influenced by mortal reality. However, as one moves deeper into the plane, logic dissolves completely and dreams become an endless whirlwind of imagination, memory and desire.
