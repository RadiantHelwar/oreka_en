# White Sky

The White Sky is a phenomenon that emerged during the era of the [[Lore/Cosmology/The Rot\|The Rot]] Those lands that succumbed to the Rot saw their skies turn pale and colourless, and the life beneath those unkind skies dry up and turn to white dust.

It had not been seen since, though evil tongues say that in the confines of Tomoe, Trabia and Serra, sometimes the sky ceases to be blue, for a few hours, and the evenings are pale.
