# The Rot

For nearly a century, Oreka went through a period of desolation known as the **Podredom**. The skies turned pale and opaque, a sign known as the **Decay**. [[Lore/Cosmology/Cielo Blanco\|Cielo Blanco]] and the fertility of the land declined. Many creatures were deformed, and food shortages brought entire kingdoms to the brink of war. It was an age of hunger, fear and despair.

According to the historians of the **Church of Aureon**, the Rot was caused by the [[Lore/Ancient Past/Four Fiend Kings\|Four Fiend Kings]] who, before being imprisoned by Aureon, managed to corrupt the heart of Ilios. Their influence took thousands of years to take effect, and eventually sickened the Tree: it caused diseases, catastrophes and changes in the fauna, leaving scars that persisted for generations.

## The Restoration

Aureon's divine intervention resolved the crisis. His power, channelled through the faith of his followers, purified Oreka and restored the fertility of the fields. Aureon removed the battered tree, and replaced it with a luminous tree of golden light, as incorruptible as it was majestic. With its appearance above the skies of Glastra, the faithful realised that their prayers had been answered and that the salvation of Oreka had been granted.

Thanks to this action, the Rot was almost completely reversed. The affected regions began to recover, the skies returned to their natural colour, and the deformed creatures ceased to proliferate.

## Aftermath and Teachings

Although the Rot ended, many lands retained scars from that period. The Church teaches that sustained and devout faith was the key to overcoming the crisis, and that as long as devotion to Aureon remains, Oreka will continue to be protected. The story of the Rot became a moral lesson: the salvation of the world depends on the faith and unity of its peoples.

Scholars, for their part, try to document and understand these events, but for the general population, the explanation is clear and simple: faith saved Oreka, and Aureon's divine protection is eternal.

The Lakua region in the centre of Glastra and the huge Kentron Lake exist today where the original, physical Tree of Ilios once stood. The whole valley is the footprint that Ilios left behind when he disappeared.
