# Astral Plane

The Astral Plane is one of the strangest planes of existence. Unlike other planes, where physical laws are altered or exaggerated, the Astral Plane is governed by a fundamental principle: _consequence precedes cause_.

This reversal of causal logic has numerous implications, but three dominate the experience of those who visit this plane.

## Information precedes matter

On the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]], information about an object can be observed and studied after the object exists. An apple can be seen, described, and understood.

On the Astral Plane, the opposite is true.

If a mind can gather all the information necessary to describe an apple, then the apple exists. If the information for a drop of water is gathered, a drop of water appears. Matter is simply an inevitable consequence of information.

## Time is a prey, not a predator.

The consequences of actions appear **before** the actions occur. From the visitor’s perspective, time seems to flow backwards, although the reality is far more complex than a simple temporal reversal.

For this reason, the Astral Plane is the main subject of study for many seers and oracles. It is impossible to see the future with absolute certainty, but the plane allows one to observe the potential consequences of events that have not yet occurred. Those able to interpret these visions can deduce what is likely to happen in the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]].

Creatures originating from other planes experience a peculiar effect upon entering the Astral Plane. Their nature attempts to move forward in time normally, while the plane pushes them in the opposite direction. The result is a state of temporal suspension: they are frozen, unable to move forward.

## Subjectivity can become objective.

Because information dominates matter, the thoughts and beliefs of individuals have a direct impact on the surrounding reality.

A sufficiently disciplined mind can alter the environment, create objects, or even generate complex structures through concentration alone. Those with extraordinary talent have gone so far as to create entire realities born from pure imagination.

Settlements in the outer regions of the Astral Plane—where the laws of the Astral Plane mingle with those of the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]]—exist in a delicate balance. There, time does not pass for its inhabitants, yet neither does it completely paralyse them.

However, those who remain too long in this liminal state find that leaving the plane has severe consequences. Returning to the Mundane Plane forces their bodies to account for all the time they have not lived, often with fatal results.

Those trapped in this predicament have only two options:
to leave and die, or delve deeper into the Astral Plane until they either become true natives or remain forever frozen between two opposing streams of time.
