# Astral Plane

The Astral Plane is one of the strangest planes of existence. Unlike other planes, where physical laws are altered or exaggerated, the Astral Plane is governed by a fundamental principle: consequence precedes cause.

This reversal of causal logic has numerous implications, but three of them dominate the experience of those who visit this plane.

## Information precedes matter

On the mundane plane, information about an object can be analysed and studied after the object exists. An apple can be observed, described and understood.

On the Astral Plane the opposite is true.

If a mind can gather all the information necessary to describe an apple, then the apple exists. If the information of a drop of water is created, a drop of water appears. Matter is simply an inevitable consequence of information.

## Time is a prey, not a predator.

The consequences of acts appear before the acts occur. From the visitors' perspective, time seems to flow backwards, although the reality is much more complex than a simple temporal reversal.

For this reason, the Astral Plane is the destination of many seers and oracles. It is not possible to see the future absolutely, but the plane allows one to see the potential consequences of events that have not yet occurred. Those who are able to interpret these visions can deduce what is likely to happen in the future. [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]].

Creatures originating from other planes suffer a peculiar effect when they enter the Astral Plane. Their nature attempts to move forward in time in the normal way, while the plane pushes them in the opposite direction. The result is that they are suspended in a state of temporal immobility, unable to move forward, frozen.

## Subjectivity can become objective.

Because information dominates matter, the thoughts and beliefs of individuals have a direct effect on the surrounding reality.

A sufficiently disciplined mind can alter the environment, create objects or even generate complex structures simply through concentration. Those with extraordinary talent have gone so far as to create entire realities born of pure imagination.

Settlements situated in the outer regions of the Astral Plane, where the laws of the Astral Plane mingle with those of the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]] exist in a delicate balance. There, time does not pass for its inhabitants, but neither does it paralyse them completely.

However, those who remain too long in this liminal state find that they can no longer leave the plane without consequences. When they return to the World Plane, their bodies try to make up for all the time they have not lived, which often proves fatal.

Those trapped in this situation have only two options:
to go deeper and deeper into the Astral Plane until they become true natives, or to remain forever frozen between two opposing streams of time.
