# Fae Plane

The Fae Plane, also called the *Feywild*, is one of the closest planes to the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]].

If the Shadow Plane can be considered the dark reverse side of material reality, the Feeric Plane is its exaggerated and distorted reflection. Many describe it as looking at the world through a wavy mirror: recognisable in its essence, but transformed into something more intense, more alive and more unpredictable.

The physical laws of this plane largely resemble those of the mundane plane. Gravity, weather and matter function similarly, and many landscapes are clearly reminiscent of places in the material world. However, everything seems to exist in a state of emotional and sensory exaggeration. Colours are more vivid, aromas more intense, seasons more dramatic and emotions more profound.

The real difference lies in its inhabitants.

The **Fey**, especially the oldest and most powerful, possess the ability to alter the reality around them by creating personal territories known as **Demesnes**. Within these regions, the dominant Fey can control fundamental aspects of the environment: the weather, the passage of time, the distance between places, and even the emotional state of those within.

In a Demesne, a forest may bloom eternally under a perpetual spring, a castle may exist simultaneously on several hills, or a guest may find that sadness or joy invades without apparent explanation.

The fey mind operates on radically different principles from those of mortals. Fey do not understand the world in terms of good and evil, or order and chaos. Instead, they follow axioms of their own that are often incomprehensible to the creatures of the [[Lore/Cosmology/Mundane Plane\|Mundane Plane]].

Among these principles are some that are particularly dangerous for visitors:

- The word given is absolute law.
- Metaphors can become literal.
- Names have real power.
- Personality and will can shape reality.

This is why encounters with the Fey often seem absurd or arbitrary to mortals, when in fact they obey very strict rules that simply escape conventional logic.

The regions of the Feeric Plane closest to the Mundane Plane are relatively stable and recognisable, and have been visited by travellers, explorers and even temporary settlements. However, the deeper one goes into the plane, the more the whims of the great Fey dominate, until reality becomes an eternal play directed by wills that are impossible to comprehend.
