# Oreka lighthouses

The Four Great Beacons of Oreka. These ancient constructions were ancient links to the four adjacent Planes, now dormant. The [[Lore/Ancient Past/Four Fiend Kings\|Four Fiend Kings]] took possession of them and [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] decided to seal them in order to lock the Demon Kings in an eternal sleep inside. This action led to his [[Lore/Ancient Past/Apoteosis\|Apoteosis]].
