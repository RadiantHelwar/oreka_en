# Lighthouses of Oreka

The Four Great Lighthouses of Oreka. These ancient constructions were once connections to the four adjacent Planes, now dormant. The [[Lore/Ancient Past/Four Fiend Kings\|Four Fiend Kings]] took possession of them, and [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] decided to seal them, trapping the Demon Kings in an eternal slumber within. This action led to their [[Lore/Ancient Past/Apotheosis\|Apotheosis]].
