# Pneumathoi


>[!quote] Excerpt from the Treatise on Wandering Souls, by the arcanist Varen Lys, 11th century DE
"The Pneuma is neither slave nor mistress; it is mirror and echo of the soul it accompanies."

Pneumathoi, or Pneumas in common speech, are outer souls of a winged nature that exist only in the Aitherion family.

The Pneuma is born at the same time as the Aitherion, and both are said to be one and the same being. The soul of the human exists outside its body, taking the appearance of a Pneumathoi.

Most Pneumathoi take the form of birds - eagles, hawks, owls, herons - although there are cases of insects, mammals, reptiles or even normally wingless creatures, which are given impossible flight by the Pneuma. Their appearance often reflects the true personality, fears and virtues of their human companion.

These forms can sometimes be mistaken for those of a natural animal, but more often than not, they have fantastic characteristics, expressions of their human companion's true self, that make them visibly distinct:

Black feathers that seem to turn to smoke with every movement, a third eye, ghostly lights surrounding the creature, the possibilities are as different as the number of Pneumas in existence.


## Growth and Change

A Pneuma does not age physically like animals, but its appearance may subtly transform over the years, reflecting the emotional and spiritual development of its companion. A light-plumaged juvenile hawk might become darker and sterner over the years if its mate goes through sorrows or wars, while a once fierce creature might soften over time.

## Communication

Pneumathoi do not use words in the human sense. They communicate through a mixture of sensation, mental imagery and an emotional echo that only their mate fully understands. Between Aitherion and Pneuma, a fleeting glance can convey warnings, commands or affection with the precision of speech. Some in True form have demonstrated the ability to project clearer thoughts or even make themselves heard in a voice of their own, though this phenomenon is rare and usually associated with an exceptionally deep bond.


## Forms and Potential

Pneumathoi have various forms or states between which they can change throughout their lives. All Pneumathoi begin life in their Basic form, which is the form they will inhabit for most of their lives. Through introspection and affinity between Pneuma and Companion, other, greater Forms can be attained, with exponentially greater strength and potential.

- Basic Form** - Small and manageable. Their powers are modest, useful for everyday life or as scout companions. Many usually perch on the shoulders of their companions, though some are too large for this, even in this, their lower form. Their powers are on par with those of lower-ranked spells.
- Released Form - The Pneumathoi adopts a battle form much larger than their Basic Form. In this form they can often act as mounts for their Companions, and their specific powers are more apparent. Each pneuma has a power according to its owner and its own nature: Some have sonic attacks, some exhale fire, some are good with magic, some are able to heal wounds, multiply or control the weather. Their powers are comparable to mid-range magic and spells.
- Royal Form - Only a few individuals are able to commune with their Pneumas to the extent that this form is possible for them. It is a symbol of bonding between Companion and Pneuma that is difficult to achieve. In this form, boundaries are difficult to deal with. The Pneuma expresses a highly evolved form and powers compared to its Released form. A Pneuma capable of exhaling fire in Liberated form could perhaps become pure flames or set fire to the sky itself. A Pneuma known for its brute strength could become a titan capable of rivalling the greatest Titans, a Pneuma capable of Healing could maintain and restore the health of everyone around it by its mere presence, etc. Its powers are on par with the most complex and highest level of magic.

## Relationship with the Aitherion

In the Aitherion lineage, the Pneuma is seen as an extension of family heritage and a symbol of legitimacy.

## Legendary Pneumathoi

Among the ancient records and songs, some Pneumathoi have earned an eternal place in the memory of Oreka:
- Skyfýlax "The Infinite Wing"- A three-tailed silver eagle of colossal size, capable of covering the entire Palace of Ragged Clouds with its wings, who accompanied Alkon the Righteous. It was said that its vocalisations could exert the power to destroy a valley if it so desired, or could be so gentle as to lull a baby to sleep.
- Nyxarion "The Azure Inferno" - A gigantic raven that flew in blue flames. He served Vaedra Aitherion, ruthless strategist, and was last seen flying over the fields of a forgotten land on the night before its destruction.
- Lúmenar "The Grace of Aureon"- A golden heron who accompanied Erian Aitherion, healer and diplomat. It is said that her presence alone calmed all emotions and her song incited peace and reconciliation.

These stories are remembered with pride, although some scholars suggest that the reality of her powers may have been exaggerated over the centuries.
