# Hunger Brigade

The Hunger Brigade emerged following the internal fracturing of [[Lore/Organizations/Reconquista\|Reconquista]] after the public expulsion of [[Frath Arguul\|Frath Arguul]] and his radical faction. From its inception, the movement abandoned all diplomatic or restorationist pretensions, adopting a posture of total war without quarter until Ergo’s complete withdrawal. The Brigade has declared anyone not part of its ranks an enemy to be slain until its objectives are achieved.

The Brigade demands the expulsion of [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] from the lands of the [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Dartza\|Dartza]] and the removal of all Elves, individuals of Elven descent, and those connected to them from the territories of [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]]. The death penalty is to be imposed on any who fail to comply with these edicts.

To this end, any method that brings them closer to their objective is deemed acceptable. Collateral damage is disregarded; only the accomplishment of their aims matters. Arguul himself presides over a faction of demonologists within the Brigade, resorting to pacts and the invocation of infernal beings to advance their cause.
