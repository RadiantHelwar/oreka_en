# Hunger Brigade

The Hunger Brigade came into being as a result of the internal fracturing of [[Lore/Organizations/Reconquista\|Reconquista]] following the public expulsion of [[Frath Arguul\|Frath Arguul]] and its radical wing. Since its inception, the movement has abandoned any diplomatic or restorationist pretensions, adopting a posture of all-out war without quarter until Ergo's complete withdrawal. The Brigade has declared anyone who is not part of the Brigade to be an enemy to be slaughtered, until its objective is achieved.

The Brigade demands the expulsion of [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] of the land of the [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Dartza\|Dartza]] and the removal of all Elves, individuals of Elven blood and those connected with them, from the lands of [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]] The death penalty shall be imposed on those who fail to comply with these provisions.

To this end, any method that brings them closer to their objective is valid. Collateral damage is not taken into account, only objectives accomplished, and Arguul himself presides over a faction of demonologists within the Brigade itself, resorting to pacts and invocation of infernal beings for use in their cause.
