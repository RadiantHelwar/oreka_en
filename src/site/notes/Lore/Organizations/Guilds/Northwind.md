## The White Shield

**Emblem**: a stylised silver owl on a mother-of-pearl background.
**Primary Colour**: Mother-of-pearl
**Secondary Colour**: Silver
**Headquarters**: [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas Capital\|Adamas Capital]] in [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]].
**Origin**: Born to refugees from the [[Faro Astrikos\|Faro Astrikos]] during the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]]
**Composition**: Varied, but dragonborn, humans and goliath abound.
**Organisation**: Governed by the High Circle (7 prominent members, usually arcanists).
**Style**: Focused on magic, knowledge and research. Their apprentices make their debut in war before accepting commissions.
**Speciality**: Arcane magic, research, magical contracts, defence against the supernatural.

Cierzo is a group focused on arcane magic. It is based in [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas Capital\|Adamas Capital]] on the continent of [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]. Its emblem is a stylised silver owl on a mother-of-pearl background. It was born as an independent group of refugees from the [[Faro Astrikos\|Faro Astrikos]] when it was taken over by the Demon King, and travelled north during the Wave of Darkness to assist in the battle against [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia]]. There they were officially formed as a mercenary group and took the name **Cierzo** in honour of their benefactors.

They are closely linked to the royal house of [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]] and they have an open-ended contract with them to keep the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Inmortal Army\|Inmortal Army]] always on the other side of [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|pagos]]. Its rookies are first introduced to the front line of battle, supervised by experts, and are not allowed to take contracts until their worth is proven. Its members travel the world taking all kinds of contracts, but specialise in all things arcane and research.

Their leadership is the **High Circle**, a group of seven of their most powerful members, usually _mages_ or _artificers_ (although their ranks include an Arcane Knight and a former pirate). Its members are divided into different teams and are charged with different tasks according to their abilities. Cierzo attracts a diverse range of races, but given the location of its headquarters, Dragonborn, Humans and Goliaths abound.
