# Northwind

## The White Shield

**Emblem**: a stylised silver owl on a mother-of-pearl background.  
**Primary Colour**: Mother-of-pearl  
**Secondary Colour**: Silver  
**Headquarters**: [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas Capital\|Adamas Capital]] in [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]  
**Origin**: Formed by refugees from the [[Astrikos Lighthouse\|Astrikos Lighthouse]] during the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]]  
**Composition**: Varied, though Dragonborn, Humans and Goliaths are common.  
**Organisation**: Governed by the High Circle, a council of seven prominent members, usually arcanists.  
**Style**: Focused on magic, knowledge and research. Apprentices are introduced to combat before taking on independent commissions.  
**Specialty**: Arcane magic, research, magical contracts, and defence against the supernatural.

Northwind is a group specialised in arcane magic. Its headquarters is in [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas Capital\|Adamas Capital]] on the continent of [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]. Its emblem depicts a stylised silver owl on a mother-of-pearl background.

It was formed by refugees from the [[Astrikos Lighthouse\|Astrikos Lighthouse]] when the Fiend King [[Izarra\|Izarra]] seized it, travelling north to [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]]. Ages later, during the Wave of Darkness, they assisted in the battle against [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia]]. There they officially established themselves as a mercenary company and adopted the name **Northwind** in honour of their benefactors.

The group maintains a close relationship with the royal house of [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]], holding an ongoing contract to keep the [[Immortal Army\|Immortal Army]] at bay beyond [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos]]. New recruits are introduced directly to the front lines under expert supervision and may only accept contracts once their abilities are proven.

Leadership rests with the **High Circle**, composed of seven of the most capable members, typically _mages_ or _artificers_ (though ranks include an Arcane Knight and a former pirate). Members are organised into teams according to their skills, carrying out tasks suited to their strengths. Northwind attracts a diverse range of races, but Dragonborn, Humans and Goliaths are the most common due to the location of their headquarters.
