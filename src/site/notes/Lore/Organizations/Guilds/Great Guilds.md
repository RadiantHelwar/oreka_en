A fifth organisation is unofficially considered a guild and is large and powerful enough to rival the Four Great Guilds on its own. This is [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]], a criminal organisation whose influence reaches across Oreka and all social strata. Naturally, it is not recognised by the Guild Association or by any government, and its members are not referred to as mercenaries. Nevertheless, it is included here due to its notoriety.

## The Guild Association

The **Guild Association** is the body that formally regulates, supervises, and recognises guilds throughout Oreka. Its primary goal is to ensure that mercenary activities are carried out under minimum standards of safety, transparency, and legality, protecting both the mercenaries and the clients who hire them.

Its main functions include:

- **Guild registration:** Any group of mercenaries can register with the Association, gaining official recognition. This facilitates accreditation across different regions and ensures that the guild’s contracts are valid and respected beyond their local borders.

- **Standards enforcement:** The Association establishes minimum standards of conduct, safety, and operation for registered guilds. While each guild maintains its own internal rules, they must comply with the Association’s basic standards to obtain or retain recognition.

- **Conflict arbitration:** Acts as a mediator in disputes between guilds or between guilds and clients, preventing conflicts from escalating into unnecessary confrontations.

- **Information network:** Maintains databases of guilds, contracts, missions, and mercenary reputations, allowing information to circulate reliably across regions and guilds.


Although the Association does not compel anyone to join a guild, official recognition is highly valued: many important contracts and high-risk jobs are only accessible through accredited guilds. Furthermore, the Association is the authority that officially certifies members of both [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Free Guilds\|Free Guilds]] and [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]], facilitating their mobility and access to opportunities throughout Oreka.
