## The Dark Guild

### [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]]
[! [velo-logow-250.png](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/velo-logow-250.png) ](https://oreka-eight.vercel.app/trasfondo/organizaciones/gremios/el-velo/)

A fifth organisation is unofficially considered a guild, and is large and powerful enough to equal in power the four major guilds alone. We speak of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]] The guild is a criminal organisation that spreads its tentacles throughout Oreka, through all strata. Obviously, it is not a guild recognised by the [[Asociación de Gremios\|Asociación de Gremios]] It is not a member of the UN, nor by any government, and its members are not called mercenaries, but it is important to include it here because of its notoriety.
