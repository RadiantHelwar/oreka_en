# Guilds

Throughout Oreka there are numerous guilds of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Mercenaries\|Mercenaries]]. These associations bring together individuals who choose to work under a common structure. Guilds organise and assign work, which can range from small tasks to full-scale military campaigns, and sometimes include research or development projects.

Guilds fall into two categories:

- [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]]
- [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Free Guilds\|Free Guilds]]

The former are the best-known, largest, and most powerful, usually operating on a continental scale. The latter tend to be smaller, local, and transactional, with small groups of mercenaries banding together under a guild name to participate in contracts.

Each guild sets its own rules: some charge fees or take a percentage from members, while others offer better contracts in exchange for membership. There is no restriction on belonging to more than one guild, aside from the internal regulations of each; in fact, many individuals are affiliated with one of the [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]] and a personal [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Free Guilds\|Free Guild]].

Registering a new guild is generally quick and easy, usually free of charge, although this may vary by region. Once registered, a guild is recognised throughout Oreka thanks to the Guild Association.

Guilds also serve as a form of authentication or proof of membership when required to undertake certain contracts or work.
