# Leaf

![hoja2.png|350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/hoja2.png)

## The Green Shadow

**Emblem**: Maroon outline of a leaf on a green background.  
**Main Colour**: Green  
**Secondary Colour**: Garnet  
**Headquarters**: [[Cibele\|Cibele]] in [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]] ([[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]])  
**Origin**: Founded following the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]], rumoured to have been established by an Adventurer from the group of [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]].  
**Composition**: Many elves, halflings, gnomes, firbolgs and humans.  
**Organisation**: Mixed leadership: a Leader and a council called the Forum. Organised in small, horizontal cells.  
**Style**: Scouts, druids and guerrilla fighters. Small, flexible, and horizontally organised.  
**Specialty**: Monster hunting and intervention, ambushes, archery (Oreka’s finest archers).

Leaf is a much more recent group, emerging after the end of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]]. Its emblem is a maroon leaf outlined on a green background.

They are smaller in number than Glaukos and consist mostly of explorers, druids, and those skilled in the wild, though all kinds of members are welcome. They take on a wide range of tasks but specialise in monster hunting and intervention, precision attacks, and guerrilla tactics. They are renowned across Oreka for having the finest archers, even envied by state armies.

Their leadership is complex: they have a Leader and a council called “the Forum”, each with distinct responsibilities, ruling together. Members are organised into small, autonomous cells, though many operate independently, travelling and accepting assignments as convenient. Their ranks include many elves, but also halflings, gnomes, firbolgs and humans. Their headquarters are in [[Cibele\|Cibele]], in [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]], [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]].
