# Leaf

! [350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/hoja2.png)

## The Green Shadow

**Emblem**: Maroon outline of a leaf on a green background.
**Main colour**: Green
**Secondary Colour**: Garnet
**Headquarters**: [[Cibele\|Cibele]] in [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]], ( [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] )
**Origin**: Founded following the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]] According to rumours, by an Adventurer from the group of [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]].
**Composition**: Many elves, halflings, gnomes, firbolgs and humans.
**Organisation**: Mixed leadership: a Leader and a council called the Forum. Structure in very horizontal cells.
**Style**: Scouts, druids and guerrilla fighters. Horizontal, small and flexible organisation.
**Specialty**: Monster hunting and mediation, ambushes, archery (Oreka's best archers).

Leaf is a much more recent group, emerging after the end of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]]. It is said to have been founded by one of the Adventurers from the group of [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]]. Its emblem is the outline of a leaf in garnet, outlined on a green background.

They are a less numerous group than Glaukos, and count among their ranks all kinds of members but mostly explorers, druids and those who do well in the wild. They take jobs of all kinds, but specialise in monster hunting (or mediation) and precise attack or guerrilla strategies. They are world renowned for having the best archers in Oreka, even envied by state armies. Their leadership is complex: they have. A **Leader**, and a council called "**the Forum**", each of these bodies have different capabilities and rule together. They are divided into cells in a very horizontal organisation, and many of their members are not even part of specific cells but travel and accept assignments as convenient. Their ranks have a large number of elves, but also halflings, gnomes, firbolgs and humans. Their headquarters are in [[Cibele\|Cibele]] in [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]] in [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]].
