# Glaukos

! [350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/glaukos-banner.jpg)

## The Blue Army

**Emblem**: Blue flag with a central white circle and a white circumference around it.
**Primary Colour**: Blue
**Secondary Colour**: White
**Headquarters**: [[Kyanos\|Kyanos]] south shore of the Lake [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]] ( [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] ).
**Origin**: 4th century AD [[Calendario Áureo\|Calendario Áureo]].
**Composition**: Multi-racial, but humans, dwarves, dragonborn and moon elves abound.
**Organisation**: Militaristic chain of command. Divided into legions, led by 3 to 5 admirals (never an even number).
**Style**: Professional paid army, rigid and disciplined. Capable of long campaigns, but also of small operations.
**Speciality**: Open warfare, logistics, military operations.

! [300](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/GlaukosSoldados.jpg)

Glaukos has a blue flag with a white circle and a circumference around it, also white. It is mainly a paramilitary group. They have all kinds of members, from soldiers, paladins or mages, to sappers, sorcerers and anyone who is useful and wants to be a cog in the Glaukos. Their structure is rigidly structured, they are divided into militaristic ranks and their leadership is divided between 3 or 5 admirals, depending on the era, but never an even number. Each of their legions has some freedom of action, but all obey the chain of command primarily.

They have small units of 2 or 3 members who roam the world taking jobs when they can and reporting back periodically, and even lone members. They are capable of taking on any job: protection, escorts, infiltration, even research and development, but are best known for being a paid army ready for long campaigns.

Their origin is in [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] and is based in [[Kyanos\|Kyanos]] on the southern shore of the Lake [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]] The name of the church, although it has moved through the ages. Its origin dates back to the 4th century after the adoption of the [[Calendario Áureo\|Calendario Áureo]]. They do not have a majority race selection, but seem to attract more humans, dwarves, dragonborn and moon elves than other races.
