# Glaukos

![glaukos-banner.jpg|350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/glaukos-banner.jpg)

## The Blue Army

**Emblem**: Blue flag with a central white circle surrounded by a white ring.  
**Primary Colour**: Blue  
**Secondary Colour**: White  
**Headquarters**: [[Kyanos\|Kyanos]], south shore of Lake [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]] ([[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]]).  
**Origin**: 4th century of the [[Golden Calendar\|Golden Calendar]].  
**Composition**: Multi-racial, though humans, dwarves, dragonborn and moon elves are more common.  
**Organisation**: Strict militaristic hierarchy. Divided into legions, led by 3 to 5 admirals (never an even number).  
**Style**: Professional mercenary army, rigid and disciplined. Capable of long campaigns as well as small-scale operations.  
**Speciality**: Open warfare, logistics, military operations.

---

![GlaukosSoldados.jpg|300](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/GlaukosSoldados.jpg)

Glaukos is primarily a paramilitary group. Its members range from soldiers, paladins and mages to sappers, sorcerers, and anyone useful who wishes to become a cog in the Glaukos machine. Each legion enjoys some autonomy, but all follow the chain of command. Leadership is divided among 3 to 5 admirals, depending on the era, but never an even number.

Small units of 2 or 3 members travel the world taking on contracts and reporting periodically, and even lone members operate independently. They are capable of performing any task—escort, protection, infiltration, research, or development—but they are best known as a paid army ready for prolonged campaigns.

Originating in [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]], Glaukos’ headquarters are in [[Kyanos\|Kyanos]] on the southern shore of Lake [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]], although they have moved over the ages. While multi-racial, humans, dwarves, dragonborn and moon elves are the most frequently found among their ranks.
