# Guilds

Throughout Oreka there are a number of guilds of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Mercenaries\|Mercenaries]]. These associations bring together combatants and non-combatants who decide to work under a common structure. Guilds organise and assign work, which can range from small tasks to full-blown warfare, and sometimes include research or development.

Guilds fall into two categories:
- [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]]
- [[Gremios Libres\|Gremios Libres]]

The former are the best known, largest and most powerful, usually operating on a continental scale. The latter tend to be smaller, local, often transactional, with small groups of mercenaries banding together under the guild name to participate in contracts.

Each guild sets its own rules: some demand dues or percentages from their members, others offer better contracts in exchange for membership. There is no rule against being part of more than one guild, beyond the internal rules of each guild; in fact many people are affiliated with one of the guilds. [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]] apart from its [[Gremios Libres\|Gremio Libre]]. 

Registering a new guild is a quick and easy process, usually free of charge, although this may vary by region.

Guilds also serve as a back-up where authentication or membership is required in order to work.
