# Red Fang

! [350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/colmillo-banner.png)
## The Red Tide

**Emblem**: A white fang with a crimson background.
**Primary Colour**: Red
**Secondary Colour**: White
**Headquarters**: [[Regions/Serra/Rhanok\|Rhanok]] archipelago of [[Regions/Serra/Serra\|Serra]].
**Origin**: A confederation of tribes, clans and brotherhoods, unified under circumstantial leadership.
**Composition**: Mostly greenskins and beastmen, plus goliath, zaharak, kobolds and humans.
**Organisation**: The most powerful clan imposes its leader as guild leader. Honour system that determines promotion or demotion in quarterly "Promotion" ceremonies.
**Style**: Violent, direct, barbaric in appearance, but with a strong internal code of respect and honour.
**Specialty**: Brutal combat, dangerous and simple missions, physical power in massive numbers.

Red Fang is the guild with the most members. Their symbol is a red fang on a white background (or vice versa). Its members are mostly "Greenskins" (Orcs, Goblins, Bugbears, Hobgoblins...), and "Beastmen" (minotaurs, centaurs, ratkin...), and strangely enough: Goliath, Zaharak and Kobolds. Although, as everywhere, there are also a lot of humans.

Their organisation is somewhat tenuous and fragile. They are actually a collection of different tribes, clans and "fraternities", and the leader of the most powerful at the time is the leader of the Red Fang. As a consequence they have many internal wars. The current leader (an orc) has held his position for 27 years, which is remarkable.

Many consider the Red Fang to be a bunch of barbarian savages, and they are not far wrong, so they tend to take simple jobs where their strength comes to the fore. But they are much more than savages, and under their current leadership they have turned Red Fang into one of the Big 4.

They have an internal code of honour that all members must follow. A member owes respect to the sponsor who invites or accepts them into the guild, and rises through the ranks by earning "Honours" through their work or by earning the respect of their peers. Every 3 months, clans hold "Promotions" and those who have sufficient Honours among their peers or superiors, move up in rank. Likewise, loss of honour can result in loss of rank or expulsion from the guild.

Its headquarters are at [[Regions/Serra/Rhanok\|Rhanok]] the largest island in the mainland / archipelago of [[Regions/Serra/Serra\|Serra]].
