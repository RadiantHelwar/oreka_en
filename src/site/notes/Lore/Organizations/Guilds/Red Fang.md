# Red Fang

![colmillo-banner.png|350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/colmillo-banner.png)

## The Red Tide

**Emblem**: a white fang on a crimson background  
**Primary Colour**: Red  
**Secondary Colour**: White  
**Headquarters**: [[Regions/Serra/Rhanok\|Rhanok]], archipelago of [[Regions/Serra/Serra\|Serra]]  
**Origin**: A confederation of tribes, clans and brotherhoods, unified under circumstantial leadership  
**Composition**: Predominantly greenskins and beastmen, with some Goliaths, Zaharak and Kobolds, as well as humans  
**Organisation**: The leader of the most powerful clan assumes leadership of the guild. A system of Honours determines promotions or demotions in quarterly “Promotion” ceremonies  
**Style**: Violent, direct, barbaric in appearance, but governed by a strong internal code of respect and honour  
**Specialty**: Brutal combat, straightforward but dangerous missions, overwhelming physical strength in large numbers

Red Fang is the guild with the largest membership. Its emblem depicts a white fang on a crimson background (or vice versa). Most members are “Greenskins” (Orcs, Goblins, Bugbears, Hobgoblins…) and “Beastmen” (Minotaurs, Centaurs, Ratkin…), with some Goliaths, Zaharak and Kobolds. Humans are also well represented.

The guild’s organisation is somewhat loose. It is essentially a collection of tribes, clans and brotherhoods, with the leader of the strongest clan at any given time becoming guildmaster. This structure has historically caused internal conflicts, though the current leader (an Orc) has held power for 27 years, which is noteworthy.

Many view Red Fang as a band of barbarian savages, and this reputation is not unfounded. They often accept simple contracts that showcase their strength. However, under their current leadership, Red Fang has grown into one of the four [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]].

All members follow an internal code of honour. Respect is owed to the sponsor who invited or accepted them into the guild, and ranks are earned by accumulating “Honours” through work or peer recognition. Every three months, clans conduct “Promotion” ceremonies: those with sufficient Honours among their peers or superiors advance in rank. Conversely, loss of honour may result in demotion or expulsion.

The guild is based in [[Regions/Serra/Rhanok\|Rhanok]], the largest island in the archipelago of [[Regions/Serra/Serra\|Serra]].
