# The Veil

! [350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/velo-logo.png)
## The Darkest Shadow

**Emblem**: Three curved lines opening at an angle, crossed by a horizontal one.
**Primary Colour**: Black
**Secondary Colour**: Red
**Nature**: criminal organisation based in the city of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Inika\|Inika]] on the island of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]].
**Headquarters**: the bar [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Ecstasy\|Ecstasy]] a place as well known for its pleasures as for the shadows that lurk in its nooks and crannies.
**Current leadership**: [[PNJs/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]] "The Red One, a moon elf who has brought the Veil to heights of power unseen in its thousand-plus-year history.
**Reputation**: The Veil is no ordinary band of thugs. Their military, political and economic power is on par with the four largest "legal" Guilds in Oreka. If these four were to unite, they might be able to stand up to Selandra. Until then, the Veil moves with almost total impunity.
**Method: discretion, cunning and measured violence. Their name inspires fear because wherever there is a secret, a shady transaction or an untraced murder, the Veil is usually behind it.

The Veil. None other than Oreka's criminal underworld, hierarchical, organised... and feared. Obviously, they don't control **all** the crime in the world, but they do control much of organised crime. Those outside their ranks are careful not to get in their way or antagonise their _Agents_.... Their base is on the island of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]]. A two-day boat trip to the southern mainland of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] The island, which may once have been a country, has centuries ago become the refuge of the underworld, taken over by the organisation as its own. Even so, there are ordinary citizens and islanders living there, so life remains somewhat normal. [[PNJs/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]] or _La Roja_ as it is known, prefers order to chaos, and the country has its own laws... although they are not the same as in the rest of the world.

What has allowed the Veil to thrive is not only its power, but that its leader is ruthless... and reasonable. If you meet _The Red One_ in her office, unless you openly provoke her, you will come out alive and intact. That is much more than could be said for previous leaders of the [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Inika\|Inika]]. Whatever you deal with her can go right or wrong, but Selandra always reinforces the logic of her mandate: there is no violence for violence's sake. Violence exists for a reason, and Selandra handles it with precision: a lot of it, but calculated. Maintaining a policy of "safe negotiations" has allowed her to become the most feared and respected leader in Oreka.

Her symbol is three wavy lines sprouting from a point and opening at right angles, cut by a horizontal stroke. Black and red, if it had colour, would be enough to instil respect and fear.
