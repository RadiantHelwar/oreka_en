# Mercenaries

! [350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/mercenarios.jpeg)

A mercenary is any individual who takes on jobs for reward: caravan protection, exterminating monsters, capturing criminals, even large military campaigns or research tasks, or small tasks such as tending a garden or making a delivery; every kind of need has a mercenary who can fill it. No formal affiliation or authentication is required to call oneself a mercenary; simply plying the trade, either independently or through contract positions, is enough.

Some jobs or regions may require authentication. Authentication is free in much of Oreka, but may require a bond in some regions; once obtained, it is recognised in much of Oreka.

Many mercenaries proceed to affiliate themselves with some [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Gremios\|Gremio]] to enjoy the benefits of this, but it is not strictly necessary.


In any case, these individuals always receive the title of _Mercenary_, never _Adventurer_. The title of adventurer is exclusively linked to [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] and their group, and only public recognition can give it.
