# Mercenaries

![mercenarios.jpeg|350](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Gremios/mercenarios.jpeg)

A mercenary is any individual who takes on jobs for reward: caravan protection, exterminating monsters, capturing criminals, even large military campaigns or research tasks, or small tasks such as tending a garden or making a delivery; every kind of need has a mercenary who can fill it. No formal affiliation or authentication is required to call oneself a mercenary; simply plying the trade, either independently or through contract positions, is enough.

Some jobs or regions may require authentication. Authentication is free in much of Oreka, but may require a bond in some regions; once obtained, it is recognised in much of the world.

Many mercenaries proceed to affiliate themselves with some [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Guilds\|Guild]] to enjoy the benefits of this, but it is not strictly necessary. Additionally, it is common for mercenaries to hold membership in two guilds: a smaller, personal [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Free Guilds\|Free Guild]] with their closest companions, and one of the [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]] to access their job boards and perks. In smaller guilds, different members often hold secondary affiliations with various Great Guilds to collectivelly gain access to all available opportunities.


In any case, these individuals always receive the title of _Mercenary_, never _Adventurer_. The title of adventurer is exclusively linked to [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Sera\|Sera]] and their group, and only public recognition can award it.
