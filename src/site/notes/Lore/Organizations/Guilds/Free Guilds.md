# Free Guilds

Throughout Oreka, numerous smaller guilds operate independently from the Four [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]]. These are the **Free Guilds**: associations of mercenaries who come together voluntarily to work under a common name, usually on a local or regional level.

Unlike the Great Guilds, Free Guilds tend to be smaller, flexible, and often transactional. They can range from small groups of friends banding together to take on contracts, to specialised guilds with unique skills or resources.

## Characteristics of Free Guilds

- **Independence:** Free Guilds are self-governing and set their own internal rules. They may choose to affiliate with a Great Guild for access to additional contracts and resources, but membership is never mandatory.

- **Flexibility:** These guilds can adapt quickly to changing circumstances. Members may join or leave freely, and multiple Free Guilds often collaborate to fulfil larger or more complex contracts.
   
- **Local focus:** While Great Guilds operate on a continental scale, Free Guilds usually maintain a regional presence. Their reputation is often built on personal trust and demonstrated skill rather than widespread notoriety.

- **Dual affiliation:** Many mercenaries are members of both a Free Guild and a Great Guild. This allows them to maintain a close-knit group of trusted colleagues while accessing the broader opportunities and benefits of the larger guild network.


## Guild Registration and Recognition

Even though Free Guilds are independent, they can register with the Guild Association to obtain official recognition. Registration offers several benefits:

- Access to official contracts and work portfolios across Oreka.
- Legal protection and credibility in transactions with clients and other guilds.
- Membership recognition that facilitates mobility and collaboration with other guilds.

Registration is usually free and straightforward, though some regions may require a small deposit or proof of legitimacy. Once registered, a Free Guild is recognised throughout Oreka and can operate openly in most territories.
