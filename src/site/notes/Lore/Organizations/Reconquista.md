# Reconquista

A group of disillusioned ex-servicemen, abandoned by [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]] following the declaration of the [[Última Tregua\|Última Tregua]] with [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] twenty years ago, signed on the condition of maintaining the _Status Quo_ according to which [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] the region of the [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Dartza\|Dartza]] The war was also a result of a de-escalation of arms that meant that many soldiers lost their jobs without many other options for survival in the impoverished Kostakt after the war.

Many of these soldiers left to fend for themselves ended up succumbing to starvation and becoming bandits in their own lands, hated and disowned by their own people, and persecuted by the Kostakt forces, until former commander [[Alhor Stammets\|Alhor Stammets]] A half-Elf, a half-Elf, gathered a few of his former subordinates and formed Reconquista.

The goal of Reconquista is primarily to regain the Dartza. Stammets never agreed to surrender, he always believed that they could drive the Ergo Elves from their lands with a little more support from the government, or even by requesting help from neighbouring countries. Therefore, Reconquista uses any means at its disposal to continue hostilities towards the Ergo invaders, and as a secondary goal, its intention is to overthrow the current government of Kostakt to implement one that will not cede its lands to the enemy.

However, 20 years have passed, and the Dartza is still under Elven control. Reconquista has lost much of its original momentum. The original soldiers are older, and few of the young join. In addition, the people of the Dartza are not easy to convince to join the fight, as many appreciate the quality of life Ergo has given them, and today's youth have grown up in an occupied Dartza, unfamiliar with the former state of the region.

This has led to a loss of power for Stammets, and the rise of a more desperate factor, led by [[Frath Arguul\|Frath Arguul]] Arguul, a Leonin, a former inhabitant of the Dartza, whose hatred of invaders and elves in general makes him believe that anything goes, as long as it serves to drive the elves out of his homeland. Arguul and his group resort to the most dastardly tactics, and regard members of the Dartza who do not wish to join the rebellion as enemies, not caring about collateral damage. Many of them have been known to resort to the use of infernal pacts.

Stammets has expelled Frath Arguul and his ilk from Reconquista in a public statement declaring them enemies, and Arguul has responded in kind, with a communique naming his new faction [[Lore/Organizations/Hunger Brigade\|Hunger Brigade]] and declaring that until Ergo leaves the Dartza, and all elves leave Kostakt, anyone who is not with the Brigade is an enemy.
