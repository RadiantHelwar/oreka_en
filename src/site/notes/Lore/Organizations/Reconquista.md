# Reconquista

Reconquista is a group of disillusioned ex-soldiers, abandoned by [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]] following the signing of the [[Last Truce\|Last Truce]] with [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] twenty years ago. The truce maintained the _Status Quo_, according to which [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] retained control of the recently conquered [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Dartza\|Dartza]] region, and included a de-escalation of arms that left many soldiers unemployed, with few options for survival in the impoverished post-war Kostakt.

Many of these former soldiers, left to fend for themselves, succumbed to starvation and became bandits in their own lands, despised and disowned by their communities, and hunted by Kostakt forces. Eventually, former commander [[Alhor Stammets\|Alhor Stammets]], a half-Elf, gathered a few of his loyal subordinates and founded Reconquista.

The primary goal of Reconquista is to reclaim the Dartza. Stammets never accepted the truce; he believed that with slightly more support from the government—or assistance from neighbouring countries—the Ergonian Elves could be driven from their lands. As such, Reconquista uses any means at its disposal to continue hostilities against the Ergo invaders. A secondary objective is to overthrow the current Kostakt government and install one that will never cede territory to the enemy.

Two decades later, however, the Dartza remains under Elven control. Reconquista has lost much of its original momentum: the original soldiers have aged, few young recruits join, and the people of the Dartza are reluctant to rise in rebellion, many having come to appreciate the quality of life provided under Ergo’s administration. Today’s youth, raised under occupation, are largely unfamiliar with the region’s former state.

This decline in influence led to the rise of a more radical faction, led by [[Frath Arguul\|Frath Arguul]], a Leonin and former Dartza native. Arguul’s hatred of the invaders and Elves drives him to believe that any method is justified if it serves to expel them. He and his followers employ ruthless tactics and regard Dartza citizens who refuse to join the rebellion as enemies, disregarding collateral damage and frequently resorting to infernal pacts.

In response, Stammets publicly expelled Frath Arguul and his followers from Reconquista, declaring them enemies. Arguul replied in kind, establishing his new faction, the [[Lore/Organizations/Hunger Brigade\|Hunger Brigade]], and declaring that until Ergo leaves the Dartza and all Elves are expelled from Kostakt, anyone not aligned with the Brigade is an enemy.
