# Tieflings

Tieflings are individuals marked by the influence of the **Evil Planes**, particularly the **Nine Hells**, **the Abyss** or other infernal or demonic realities. This influence often manifests itself through blood, magic or ancient pacts, leaving a visible mark on both the body and soul of the individual.

Contrary to some popular beliefs, Tieflings are not a separate species. They can be born among virtually any mortal people, retaining many of the traits of their species of origin, though transformed by their infernal heritage.

## Appearance

Tieflings are easily recognisable due to their extraplanar features: horns, which can take a variety of shapes; long, flexible, sometimes even prehensile tails; unnaturally coloured skin, such as deep red, purple, dark blue or black; eyes with no visible pupils, or with unearthly glows.

Other less common traits include prominent fangs, claw-like nails, hair that seems to move on its own, a certain scent of sulphur, forked tongues or an abnormal body temperature.

Unlike the Aasimar, whose features tend to blend harmoniously with those of their species, a Tiefling's appearance is often strange or unsettling even to those accustomed to seeing them.

## Infernal Inheritance

The appearance of a Tiefling can be due to multiple causes.

The most common is the offspring of individuals who at some point in their lineage were linked to infernal entities, either through pacts, magical corruption, or direct union with creatures of the Malign Planes.

However, this inheritance may lie dormant for generations before manifesting again. In some cases, two lineages with diluted traces of infernal influence may merge and unexpectedly give rise to a Tiefling.

There are also other possible origins, such as:

- Being conceived or born on a **Malign Plane**.
- Being marked by an infernal curse or pact.
- Having been exposed to large amounts of negative or abyssal energy.

On rare occasions, an individual may become a Tiefling later in life, usually as a result of rituals, pacts or extreme magical transformations.

## Variations according to species of origin

Because infernal influence can manifest in any mortal people, there are numerous variations of Tieflings that reflect their species of origin.

Some of these variations have been given specific names throughout history. Among the best known are:

- **Fey'ri**, Tieflings of elven origin whose demonic blood is traced back to ancient pacts or mixtures with abyssal entities.
- **Tanarukk**, individuals of orc ancestry transformed by demonic corruption.

Despite these differences, most scholars and travellers use the term **Tiefling** as a general category for all individuals marked by similar infernal heritages.
