# The Kitraki (Non-playable species)

**Name: Kitraki
**Type**: Humanoid (Insectoid)
**Languages**: Kitraki (buzzes, clicks and resonances), Common, Haizean

## General Description

The Kitraki are a rational, half-humanoid, half-wasp insectoid species, created - according to their own legends - by Itia, the Great Healer, from the corrupted sap that sprang forth when the Great Branch of Ilios fell four millennia ago. That which was first born as monsters ended up transforming into social, orderly and brilliant beings, thanks to the touch of their protector.

Today, the Kitraki form an advanced, eusocial civilisation, protective of their neighbours, but fearsome to anyone who does not respect their rules.

## Appearance

A typical Kitraki combines anthropomorphic features with the anatomy of a wasp:

- Bright chitin of yellow, black, ochre and reddish tones.
- Compound eyes that reflect light like faceted crystals.
- Articulated mouthparts capable of mimicking human speech.
- Six chitin-coated humanoid limbs, four upper-limbs and two lower-limbs
- Membranous wings that vibrate with a buzzing sound recognisable for miles.
- Segmented abdomen with retractable stinger.

All in all, the Kitraki are fascinating, powerful and slightly unsettling.

## Biology and Physiology

**Hydraulic movement system**: instead of muscles, they pump pressurised internal fluids to move their limbs, giving them explosive strength but peculiar movements.

**Reinforced chitin**: their "skin" functions as a lightweight natural armour.

**Moderate Eusociality**: each individual has its own personality, but all have a strong internal drive for hierarchical cooperation.

**Poisonous Stinger**: Used only in combat or rituals.

**Periods of diapause**: In times of high stress, a Kitraki may "pause" in a state of metabolic sparing without warning.

**Their mind is rational, but their thinking does not follow human patterns**: they understand duty as a physical principle, not a moral one.

## Culture and Identity

Although each Kitraki may have personal aspirations, their society is organised around:

- The Matrix Queen
- The Warrior Castes
- The Craftswomen and Builders
- The Wise Women

All share three central impulses:

1. To protect what is theirs.
2. To honour the Pact of Itia, which mandates them to guard the Harana rift and to help their neighbours when they ask.
3. To keep Kertutai, their home, safe, orderly and prosperous.

## Relationship with other species

Most humanoid races find it difficult to read Kitraki emotions. For them, following rules is an absolute. Mistaking a gesture can cost you your life.

Still, they are seen as:

- Reliable allies for [[Regions/Glastra/Haizea\|Haizea]] and the region of [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Lakua\|Lakua]].
- Essential guardians for those who must cross the Harana.
- Silent threats for those who try to break its laws.

## Reputation

In much of Oreka, the Kitraki are regarded as:

> "That kingdom you would never want to antagonise....
> but which you bless to have near you in times of war."
