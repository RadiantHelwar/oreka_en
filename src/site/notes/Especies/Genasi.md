# Genasi

The **Genasi** are individuals marked by the direct influence of the **Elemental Planes**, and their presence reflects the nature of those planes in their own form. Like the other Planetouched, the Genasi are not a separate species, but may be born among any mortal people, though their appearance immediately reveals their connection to elemental forces.

## Appearance

Each Genasi reflects the plane that marked it, and its body takes on physical traits, colours and textures directly linked to that element:

- **Fire Genasi:** Warm or incandescent skin, hair that looks like living flames, eyes like burning embers, and a slight heat that surrounds their body.
- **Water Genasi:** Moist or pearly skin, hair that flows like currents or waves, deep and shining eyes, and the constant sensation of coolness around them.
- **Air Genasi:** Hair and clothing that seem to move in the breeze, light and translucent skin in some areas, eyes as bright as the sky, and often a slight hissing sound that accompanies their movements.
- **Earth Genasi:** Skin textured like stone or clay, or even like gems or metals, hair and eyebrows like minerals or vegetation, deep, earthy eyes, and a solid presence that seems to make the ground tremble slightly in its wake.
- **Ice/Cold Genasi:** Pale or translucent skin, hair like frost, breath that can smoke, and an aura of cold that can be felt even from a distance.

Other less common traits may include eyes that reflect storms, visible wind around them, water droplets suspended in the air, or the ability for their hair and skin to subtly change with the season or weather.

Unlike Tieflings, whose appearance seems to intimidate or stand out, Genasi are a living mirror of the element that touches them, and their presence tends to inspire admiration, respect or wariness depending on the context.

## Elemental Origin

Genasi can arise in many ways, but all have in common direct exposure to an Elemental Plane:

- Offspring of individuals with latent elemental blood.
- Birth in close contact with an elemental plane, or at moments of magical convergence between worlds.
- Blessings from elemental entities or absorption of elemental energy in significant quantities.
- On rare occasions, post-birth transformations through ritual, accident or prolonged exposure to elemental magic.

As with the other Planeswalkers, this influence may lie dormant for generations before manifesting, and the combination of lineages can produce unpredictable results.
