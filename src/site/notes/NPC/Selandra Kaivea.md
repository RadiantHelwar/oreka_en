# Selandra Kaivea, _The Red One_.

## General Information

![Selandra.jpg|250](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/PNJs/Selandra.jpg)

**Position**: Undisputed leader of the underworld of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]].
**Personality**: Hard, cold and calculating. She is ruthless, but reasonable within the codes of her world. She prefers negotiation to useless violence, though she is happy to exercise it.
**Base of operations**: The club [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Ecstasy\|Ecstasy]], a venue for music, dance and shady business, located at [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]]. Under its lights no one dies: the place is neutral territory.
**Internal Code** ("The Red Rule"):
- Death has a purpose.
- The word given is sacred.
- In Ecstasy there is no killing.

**Influence**: Controls the flow of black trade, mercenaries, contraband and even artists. At [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]], every big business eventually passes through their hands.

---

Selandra Kaivea's name resonates in every corner of the world. They say she is over six hundred years old, but whoever sees her discovers a young, athletic, fiery-eyed elf. Her red eyes are the first thing anyone remembers about her, a trait that does not belong to her lineage and that has fuelled countless rumours: forbidden magic, a dark pact, or perhaps taken from higher entities.

Selandra was not born queen of the underworld, she made her place through blood and patience. Some six centuries ago, she appeared out of nowhere, a mere teenage elf, and defeated [[Denro\|Denro]], a goliath known for his unstoppable strength in a duel to the death for control of his band of criminals, which would come to be known as the [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]]. Instead of killing him, he plucked out his eye, forced him to swear an oath of allegiance... and gave him a replacement eye, blue as ice. Today, Denro is still by his side, a personal guardian and living proof that confronting _The Red One_ does not bring good fortune.

His dominance extends from the back alleys to the most luxurious halls of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]] and it is felt throughout Oreka. Its headquarters is [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Ecstasy\|Ecstasy]] a club where everything is bought and sold, from information to whole lives. Within those walls is their "Red Rule": no killing. Everything else is allowed.

She is not fair, but she's reasonable. She is not good, but she keeps her word. Those who deal with her soon learn that her favour is as dangerous as her anger: Selandra never forgets, neither a grievance nor a gesture of loyalty.

Evil tongues tell that she is addicted to drink, but never loses her head. And if you ever see her smile, not sardonically, but with real feeling: Run away.

Many call her simply 'The Red'. But everyone knows one thing: in [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]] nothing big happens without her permission... and in the rest of the [[Regions/Oreka\|Oreka]] nothing happens without she knowing about it and taking advantage of it.
