# Zaharak

## Biography

The Zaharak are a race of small, agile and hardy humanoid saurians whose proportions are vaguely reminiscent of ancient primordial beasts: dinosaurs. Although most belong to the small size category, there are larger, more robust and much bulkier lineages.

They are an adaptable and curious species, deeply marked by a serene spirituality and a strong sense of community. Wherever they settle, the Zaharak tend to integrate without losing their identity, forming their own neighbourhoods or small settlements close to other cultures.

According to their own beliefs, the Zaharak are not originally from Oreka, but descendants of a people who once inhabited the green moon, [[Regions/The Moons/Lehar\|Lehar]] which they call in their language _Ancestral Home_. They say that, in the distant past, a compassionate deity called **Tisara** offered them a way to leave Lehar and join the other peoples of the world. That path, described as a tangible rainbow that the goddess built with her magical ship, could only be crossed once, and to do so meant giving up forever [[Regions/The Moons/Lehar\|Lehar]].

Only a few accepted that journey, and it is from them that all the present-day Zaharak are descended.

Since then, they revere Tisara as their mother and guide, and believe that, upon death, the spirit of each Zaharak is carried back to Lehar by a rainbow of light, where it rests with its ancestors. The green moon is visible from almost anywhere in Oreka, and its constant presence is a reminder to them of their origin and their final destination.


## Settlements and populations

The Zaharak are scattered throughout Oreka, both in communities of their own and as part of settlements of other species. Although their global population is not particularly large, they are a constant and recognisable presence on almost every continent.

The largest and most influential Zaharak enclave is the [[Regions/Glastra/Tisaran Theocracy\|Tisaran Theocracy]] located on the north coast of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]]. It is an organised state with stable civil institutions and a strong cultural identity linked to the cult of Tisara. It coordinates diplomatic relations with other peoples and preserves many of the oldest traditions of the species.

On the continent of [[Regions/Tomoe/Tomoe\|Tomoe]] is the [[Tribal Community of Adal-Lehar\|Tribal Community of Adal-Lehar]], a confederation of clans with a more decentralised structure, where spiritual authority carries more weight than political authority. Their rituals and customs are especially linked to the observance and worship of [[Regions/The Moons/Lehar\|Lehar]].

There are also numerous smaller settlements scattered throughout the archipelago of [[Regions/Serra/Serra\|Serra]] The communities are generally isolated, self-sufficient and small, many of them with their own traditions.

At [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]] inhabit most of the large Zaharak. They do not usually establish permanent cities, but live in small nomadic groups known as the [[Díkaii Timoría\|Díkaii Timoría]]. They move seasonally and maintain a more austere and markedly independent lifestyle.

Lastly, it is known that in the depths of the [[Regions/Glastra/Harana\|Harana]] inhabit a number of wild and unfriendly Zharak tribes. These groups reject almost all outside contact and maintain much more primitive ways of life, alien to both the political structures of the Tisaran Republic and the more widespread tribal traditions. Their exact origins and beliefs are little known, and most accounts of them come from failed expeditions or hostile encounters. There are no known attempts at contact that have not ended in violence.

## Characteristics of the Zaharak

**Size:** Small or Medium (to be chosen at character creation).

> There are even rumours of even much larger Zaharak.

**Creature Type:** Humanoid

**Speed**: 30 feet / 9 metres

**Languages:** You can speak, read, and write Common and Zaharak Song. Zaharak Song is a tonal, resonant language composed of clicks, chirps, and whistles - other creatures can learn it as normal, but only Zaharak can produce the full resonant effects to speak it.

**Scaly Skin:** Your scales / feathers produce natural armour: as long as you are not wearing armour your AC is 13 + your Dexterity modifier. You can still use a shield.

### Build-a-Dinosaur

Choose an environmental effect, a movement option and a Natural Weapon:

#### Environmental Effects:
Whichever you choose, you may use it 1 time per short rest.
- **Thermal Vision**: As an action, you can increase blood flow to your eyes and activate ancestral thermal vision for up to 1 minute. While active, you have advantage on Perception rolls that rely on sight, and can detect creatures whose body temperature differs from their surroundings through non-magical darkness, but not through total cover.
- **Saurian Tenacity**: Whenever you would fail a Constitution save against extreme environmental effects (such as cold, heat, thin air, pressure), poison, disease, or drowning, you can re-roll the roll and keep the newer one.

#### Movement Options
- **Speedy** - Your movement speed is increased by 5 feet. Once per short rest you may cast "[Longstrider](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:longstrider)" on yourself without needing components.
- **Arboreal** - You have a climbing speed equal to your walking speed. Once per short rest you may cast the spell "[Jump](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:jump)" on yourself without needing components.
- **Aquatic** - You have a swimming speed equal to your walking speed. You can hold your breath for 10 minutes before you start drowning.
- **Flyer** - You have wings. You can glide: when you fall from at least 10 feet, you can move horizontally up to your speed and voluntarily descend up to 10 feet for every 5 feet moved. In addition, you can gain a fly speed equal to your speed for 1 minute, which you can use as many times as your proficiency bonus for long rest. (You cannot use either speed while wearing medium or heavy armor).

#### Natural Weapon Options
- **Claws:** Your unarmed claw attacks do 1d6 slashing damage and you are proficient with them. They are natural weapons and you add your Dexterity modifier to attack and damage rolls (Counts as a simple melee weapon for the purpose of feats/skills requiring a weapon).
- **Hammertail**: Your unarmed attacks with your tail do 1d6 blunt damage and you are proficient with it. The tail is a natural weapon and you add your Dexterity modifier to attack and damage rolls (counts as a simple melee weapon for the purpose of skills requiring a weapon).
- **Horns**: Your unarmed attacks with your horns do 1d4 piercing damage and you are proficient with them. It is a natural weapon and you add your Strength or Dexterity modifier to attack and damage rolls (counts as a simple melee weapon for the purpose of feats/skills requiring a weapon).
  In addition, once per turn you can use a Bonus Action to make an attack with the horns, as long as you haven't used them in your Main Action.
- **Acid Spit**: You can use the "[Acid Splash](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:acid-splash)" cantrip, Constitution is your ability for it.
- **Thunderous Voice**: You can use a special version of the "[Fire Bolt](http://dnd2024.wikidot.com/spell:fire-bolt)" cantrip called "Sonic Bolt" replacing the fire damage with sonic/thunderous. Constitution is your ability for it.
