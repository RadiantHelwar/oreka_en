# Ecstasy

The **Ecstasy** is a club located in the heart of the _Mercantile Arch_ of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Inika\|Inika]], on the island of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]]. At first glance it looks like a music and dance venue, a space of warm lights where merchants, travellers and artists gather to enjoy shows and conversations over drinks and intoxicating substances. However, beneath this façade of entertainment lies one of the island's power centres.

Ecstasy is neutral territory. Within its walls, contracts are negotiated, alliances are sealed, favours are exchanged and deals are struck that affect the entire region. In a world where many decisions are made with violence, here the word carries more weight than the sword.

No blood is shed in Ecstasy.


## The Red Rule

The operation of the venue is governed by an unbreakable principle known as **The Red Rule**, established by [[NPC/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]], nicknamed _The Red_. This rule holds that death only has meaning when it serves a clear purpose, that the word given is sacred and that within Ecstasy no one may kill.

It is not a recommendation, nor a symbolic tradition. It is an absolute law. Breaking it does not only mean making an enemy of the club, but directly defying the authority of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]]. That is why even the most dangerous individuals in [[Regions/Oreka\|Oreka]] respect the space of Ecstasy when they cross its gates.

In fact, Selandra herself offers to mediate between disputes, and can offer with full conviction the safety of those involved. It is a free offer, for the price she requires is that the discussion be in her office in the Ecstasy, in her presence, where she can listen, and participate.

## Selandra's Office

Inside the Ecstasy is the alcove from which [[NPC/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]] governs [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]] and coordinates the operations of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]]. It is neither a throne room nor a traditional office, but an intimate space carefully protected by multiple magical safeguards. These defences not only ensure her safety, but allow her to control the relationship between the space and the rest of the club.

Selandra can listen to the music in the room if she chooses, or muffle it to absolute silence. She can make her alcove visible to whomever she chooses, or keep it veiled from the eyes of others. In essence, the space responds to her will without breaking the public neutrality of Ecstasy. Thus, the place functions simultaneously as a command centre and as a social stage.

However, Selandra does not remain isolated on this strategic height. She frequently descends to the main level of the club, mingling with merchants, artists and travellers. It is not uncommon to see her sharing a drink with a lucky drunk, hearing stories first-hand or simply observing the pulse of the island. That closeness, more than any threat, is one of the reasons her authority is so strong.

## Function in Marathon

Ecstasy plays an essential role in the balance of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]]. It is the point where traders from different regions, foreign emissaries, artists, adventurers and underworld figures converge. Many transactions that never appear in official records begin or end here.

The club's existence allows rivalries to be channelled through dialogue rather than direct conflict. It also reinforces Marathon's image as a stable and attractive place for those wishing to invest, trade or simply enjoy the island.

In a sense, Ecstasy is the diplomatic and economic heart of the city.

And all this under the silent watch of Selandra.

## Presence and Reputation

Those who know the politics of Marathon know that nothing of great importance happens without passing through Ecstasy. It is not simply a leisure business: it is a space where the pulse of power is measured.

Its reputation is based on the consistency of its leadership. Selandra does not demand blind loyalty, but she does demand respect. And within the club, that respect is mutual. There is no gratuitous chaos there; there is order, even if it is a different order from the rest of the world.

Ecstasy does not rule the island, but it is one of the places that sustains its balance.
