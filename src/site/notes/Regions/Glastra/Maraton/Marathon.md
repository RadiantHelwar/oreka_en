# Marathon

_The Island of the Veil_

## General Information

- **Type**: Island-state
- **Location**: Off the coasts of Las Comarcas and Evisnos, south of the mainland of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] 
- **Capital**: [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Inika\|Inika]] 
- **De facto ruler**: [[PNJs/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]] "The Red"
- **Dominant organisation**: [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]] 
- **Legal army**: The Nacre Guard
- **Weather**: Warm, coastal, constant breezes and clear skies most of the year.

## Overview

Marathon is an island located off the coast of [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]] y [[Regions/Glastra/Evisnos\|Evisnos]], south of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]]. From the sea, it is distinguished by its white stone cities, its ever-bustling harbour and the intense glow of its buildings in the sun. It is a warm, vibrant and cosmopolitan place, where trade routes from all over the world converge.

Although at first glance it seems an idyllic destination, Marathon is primarily known for being the heart of the organised underworld of Oreka. [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]] rules here, a structured and powerful criminal organisation that controls much of the mainland's illegal trade. However, the island is not a chaotic territory: it is carefully managed, and its stability is one of the reasons for its success.

The capital is [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Inika\|Inika]], a northern coastal city, the political and economic centre of the island.

## Origin and History

Approximately six hundred years ago, Marathon was ruled by violent gangs who had ousted the legitimate rulers. The island lived in a state of constant conflict, where power changed hands by force and the population suffered the consequences.

Everything changed with the arrival of [[PNJs/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]], known as _The Red_. After defeating the most feared leader on the island, she did not establish a regime of terror, but a system based on discipline, hierarchy and clear rules. Out of this process, the Veil was born as a structured organisation.

Selandra understood that chaos was bad for business. Under her leadership, the island ceased to be a territory of scattered gangs and became a functional state where crime, far from disappearing, was integrated into a regulated system.

Since then, Marathon has prospered.

## Economy and Activity

Marathon combines legal trade and underworld activities in a calculated balance. The island has developed an economy geared as much to international trade as to entertainment and services.

Its ports receive ships from all over Oreka. The island is known for its markets, gambling houses, music venues, spices, international cuisine and trade in exotic goods. Selandra deliberately promoted this model to attract external wealth without relying on plunder.

The Veil controls the flow of black trade, but also protects legal businesses, understanding that the island's prosperity is its greatest source of power.

Nothing happens on Marathon without the Veil knowing about it.

## Government

Although real power resides with Selandra and the Veil, Marathon maintains a formal administrative structure.

[[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Inika\|Inika]] has a mayor who manages the day-to-day affairs of the city with wide autonomy, always under the supervision of Selandra. This separation allows the island to function efficiently without the need for constant intervention in every detail on her part.

Order is maintained through a combination of public laws and the so-called *Red Rule*, the internal code of the Veil, whose central principle is simple: in [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Ecstasy\|Ecstasy]] one does not kill.

[[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Ecstasy\|Ecstasy]], the club from which Selandra conducts much of her affairs is neutral territory. Within its walls, negotiations replace violence.

## Society

Most of Marathon's inhabitants are not part of the criminal world. They are fishermen, traders, artisans, artists and workers who live in relative tranquillity thanks to the stability offered by the current system.

Many value the security and prosperity that the island provides. Others are wary of the power of a criminal organisation that rules effectively. However, the previous alternative - gangs at constant war - is enough of a reminder that much of the population prefers the current order.

The island also attracts individuals seeking opportunities that would be frowned upon elsewhere, always within the limits that the Veil deems acceptable.

## Forces and Security

The official defence of the island rests with the **Nacre Guard**, an armed body that patrols the streets of Inika and the rest of the island in iridescent scale armour. Although they act as a lawful army, their ultimate allegiance is tied to the system Selandra has built.

Their role is to maintain internal stability and monitor potential external threats or foreign agents.

## Foreign Relations

Marathon maintains trade relations with multiple regions of Oreka. Its strategic location makes it a key hub for maritime trade.

The Veil avoids unnecessary conflict. Selandra prefers negotiation to open warfare, though she does not hesitate to use force when necessary. This policy has allowed the island to maintain its independence and prosperity for centuries.
