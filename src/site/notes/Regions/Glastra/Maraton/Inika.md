# Inika

_The Festival City_

## Overview

Inika is the capital of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]] and the political, economic and social heart of the island. Situated on the north coast, around a wide natural inlet, its white silhouette contrasts with the intense blue of the sea and the sky.

It is known throughout Oreka as *the Festival City*, a title earned not only for the abundance of celebrations it hosts throughout the year, but for its deeply multicultural identity. Inika is home to people from many different backgrounds, and their traditions have intertwined to make the town a living mosaic of customs, music and commerce.

Festivals are not only cultural expression: they are also economic policy. In Inika, celebrating means moving money, attracting visitors and strengthening networks of influence. Leisure is part of the system.


## Urban Structure

Inika is organised in a series of concentric arcs that surround the harbour inlet, like rings growing from the water inland. This arrangement is not only aesthetic: it reflects the social and functional hierarchy of the city.

### The Harbour Arch

This is the ring closest to the sea. This is where docks, warehouses, shipyards and loading and unloading areas are concentrated. It is a space that is always active, where goods, travellers and news come and go. The streets are wide to facilitate the transit of carts and maritime caravans.

It is the point of contact between Marathon and the rest of the world.

### The Low Arch

Behind the harbour is the Lower Arch, made up of modest but well-kept houses. Although the houses are smaller, all the architecture maintains the characteristic immaculate white of Inika. It is home to dock workers, artisans and families who sustain the daily life of the city.

Simplicity here does not imply visible poverty: cleanliness and order are part of the urban identity.

### The Merchant's Arch

This is the commercial and social heart of Inika. Here you will find shops, specialised markets, auction houses, inns, entertainment venues and casinos. Activity is constant, both day and night.

In this arc is the club [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Ecstasy\|Ecstasy]], the headquarters of the [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/The Veil\|The Veil]]. Although it is a place of shady business, within its walls there is a strict rule: it is neutral territory.

The Arco Mercantil is where the city generates much of its wealth.

### The High Arch

Higher and less travelled, the Upper Arc is home to more prestigious residences, administrative buildings and the seat of Inika's city council. From here, the civil affairs of the city are managed, always under the supervision of the political system of [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]].

It is a space of balance between the legal and the real.

### The Clouds

The innermost and highest ring is home to aristocrats and high status figures in small white palaces. Although Marathon is not a traditional kingdom, there are influential families, elite merchants and representatives of the Veil who reside in this area.

From The Clouds, the city and the sea are visually overlooked.


## Festivals and Culture

Inika celebrates festivals throughout the year. Some are religious in origin, some commercial, some cultural, and many are born out of traditions brought by foreign communities that have settled on the island.

Over time, these celebrations ceased to belong to any one group and became part of the common calendar of the city. Nowadays, almost every season has its own big festival.

The streets are filled with music, lights, parades, temporary markets and shows. To visitors, it may seem like a continuous party; to locals, it's simply the way Inika works.

## Government and Control

Although Inika has a mayor and a formal administration, the real authority in the city belongs to [[PNJs/Selandra Kaivea\|Selandra Kaivea]] and the Veil system. The coexistence between legal government and underlying power is one of the keys to the balance of the city.

While public laws are enforced on the surface, the deep order of Inika is maintained by the structural influence of the Veil.
