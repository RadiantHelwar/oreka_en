# Bronzehold

The capital of Evisnos is **Bronzehold**, a city built around the ancestral lair of the Governor.

It is a multi-layered metropolis. It stands on a broad hill, surrounded by dragonborn inhabited terraces with sumptuous palaces and well laid out streets with elegant buildings and a logical layout; underground districts dug and maintained by kobolds, chaotic and meaningless to most, but used by those who know them to cut from one side of the city to the other in a jiffy. At the top, a large open esplanade leads to the entrance of Ilmathar's cavern, the mouth of which has been framed by columns and worked stone, but never closed.

The city is designed for flight. Wide avenues, open spaces and low towers allow dragons to descend without difficulty. The structures are sturdy, designed to withstand gusts of wind, heat or electricity.

Bronzehold is both a diplomatic headquarters and a symbol of the covenant. It is where draconic meetings are held to elect Governors. Treaties are negotiated there. It is where dragonborn knights swear oaths of service.

And there, in the skies above the hill, giant silhouettes can sometimes be seen spinning in slow circles, measuring forces without yet touching.
