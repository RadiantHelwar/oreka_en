# The Shires

> [!Quote] Shirean proverb
> Water belongs to no one, but we all depend on it"_.

The Shires are a region of fertile plains crossed by a vast network of rivers, lakes and canals. They do not constitute a kingdom in the traditional sense, nor do they recognise a monarchy or high nobility. They are made up of small autonomous communities, united by pact and by a common will to cooperate.

In the eyes of powers such as [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Lakua\|Lakua]], [[Regions/Glastra/Haizea\|Haizea]], o [[Regions/Glastra/Demenor\|Demenor]], the Shires may seem like a minor land. However, their stability, river network and strong shared identity have kept them free from external domination for generations.

## Geography

The Shires extend to the south of [[Regions/Glastra/Cínere\|Cínere]], where the caudalous rivers descending from the arid canyons lose their momentum as they reach the open plain until they end at the open sea. There, the sedimentary and fertile terrain allows the riverbeds to meander, form secondary branches and lagoons.

Over the centuries, the inhabitants have widened and connected these natural waterways by means of ditches, sluices and canals, creating a veritable navigable spider's web.

Water is at once a means of transport, a natural boundary, an agricultural livelihood, and the cultural backbone of the region. 

The borders between Shires are often defined by rivers, forests or small land elevations.


## Political organisation

There are currently **thirty-one Shires**, each with its own name, origin and idiosyncrasies, and each considered an autonomous unit with its own local government.

Each Shire is usually headed by a mayor, a small council of elders, or representatives of influential families.

The Shires are bound together by the **Pact of Hospitality**, a co-operation agreement that provides for mutual defence, free river transit, mediation of disputes, and collaboration in water maintenance. 

The County Council is made up of representatives from each Shire. It meets irregularly, when one of the members convenes the Council and not all members always attend, depending on the interest they have in the issue to be discussed, or the influence they can exert on the decision. Its authority is limited and is exercised by consensus rather than by imposition. The Council does not govern the Shires: it coordinates them.

When a member of the County Council believes that there is an issue that the Council should deal with, it is that member who is responsible for convening the others and accommodating them for the duration of the decision-making process.

## Military Force

Each Shire maintains its own local watch, responsible for security and the maintenance of order.

As part of the Pact, one tenth of each Shire's forces are placed at the disposal of the Council to form what is known as the **Shire Army**: a coordinated militia, more defensive than offensive, although this is rarely enforced.

## Culture

Most Shirefolk lead simple lives: farming, fishing, river trade and crafts.

Not all desire a quiet life. Some young men seek adventure, magic or service in minor mercenary companies, though these tend to be small and local.

The shires are not home to the headquarters of great orders or famous companies, but to small groupings that act to solve regional problems.


## Foreign Relations

The Shires maintain friendly treaties with [[Regions/Glastra/Cínere\|Cínere]], [[Regions/Glastra/Evisnos\|Evisnos]] and [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]] as well as with much of Glastra. This diplomatic network, coupled with its limited expansionist interest, has helped deter attempts at conquest.

For the past twenty years, they have hosted a steady stream of refugees from Kostakt. While the reception has been mostly cordial, the prolonged arrival of new people has led to cultural tensions.

As a result, a new Shire has recently emerged, inhabited almost entirely by refugees, whose integration into the traditional Shirean fabric is still in progress.

Trade with [[Regions/Glastra/Evisnos\|Evisnos]] has for generations been remarkably close. It is known that the [[Ilmathar\|Ilmathar]] has a home in The Shires and enjoys spending time in the land of the rivers under a mortal guise.
