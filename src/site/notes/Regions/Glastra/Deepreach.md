# Deepreach

The capital of the Tisaran Theocracy stands at the exact spot where, according to Zharak tradition, the rainbow of Tisara first touched the land of Oreka.

The city is called _Deepreach_, not because of its height, but because of its depth. In its centre opens a great natural depression, a smooth and wide crater in the heart of which lies the largest known fragment of Tisaran Glass, the **Iris Shard**. It is neither erect like a tower nor carved like a statue: it emerges from the ground in an irregular, translucent form, pierced by streaks of light that seem to move slowly within it at dawn.

The city was built around the Iris Shard.

Deepreach has no high walls. Its buildings, of light stone and Prismatic Crystal reinforcements are arranged in circular terraces that slope down towards the centre. The sky can be seen from any point. The temples have no closed roofs; their columns support open frames through which light filters and fragments in soft tones.

In the cool hours of dawn, when mist rises from the jungle of the nearby valleys, the central glass refracts the light and tints the whole city in subdued colours. It is at this time that many Zaharak believe that the distance to Lehar is at its thinnest.
