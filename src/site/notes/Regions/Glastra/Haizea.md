# Haizea

- **Capital**: [[Regions/Glastra/Valtura\|Valtura]]
- **Government**: Marquisate vassal of [[Regions/Glastra/Ieró\|Ieró]]
- **Royal Household**: [[Lore/Aitherion\|Aitherion]]
- **Inhabitants**: Humans (majority), Goliath, Owlin, Aaracroka, Fauns.

Haizea was once an independent kingdom claiming descent from the first sons of Aureon. During the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]] his court supported the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Dark Lord\|Dark Lord]], confident in the inevitability of his victory. After his defeat, the subsequent purge eliminated the high nobility involved.

The only surviving nobleman of high rank at the time, Marquis _Alvaren of the High Cliffs_, swore allegiance to [[Regions/Glastra/Ieró\|Ieró]] to avoid total occupation. As a result, Haizea was downgraded from a Kingdom to a Marquisate, becoming politically subordinate.

Since then, it has ruled as a vassal state, although local pride and the desire to regain its former status are still present.

## Heraldry

- Flag: diagonally divided background of deep blue (sky) and stone grey (cliffs), crossed by a white wavy line representing the wind. In the centre, a silhouette of a golden falcon in descending flight.
- Coat of arms: blue field with a silver cliff, above it a golden falcon with outstretched wings; in the base, three white wavy lines symbolising air currents. The bell is a marquess crown, and behind the shield, two crossed spears.

## Geography and Culture

Haizea is a land of sharp cliffs, deep valleys and constant wind. Its architecture is designed to withstand the strong currents that sweep through the region, and many of its towns are built on stone terraces or are built directly into the cliffs.

It is known for:

- Its falconry and messenger birds.
- High quality minerals.
- Its mountain textiles.
- Its medicinal plants, unique in all of Glastra.

Wind is a central part of their cultural identity, both in their music and in their traditional festivals.

## Current situation

Although politically subordinate, Haizea maintains a strong regional identity. Many of its inhabitants believe that the region retains the favour of Aureon, and that it will one day regain its former status.
