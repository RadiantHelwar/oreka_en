# Lakua

Lakua is the name given to the central region of Glastra, a territory defined less by its land boundaries than by the waters of the [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]] which occupies more than half of its area. For many foreigners, Lakua is not a land with a lake, but a network of towns floating around an inland sea.

Similar in structure to [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]], Lakua is a conglomeration of autonomous city-states, each with its own laws, fleets and traditions. However, they all recognise the authority of the Central Council, whose headquarters is in [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Riza\|Riza]], on the banks of the [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]]. From there, trade routes, fishing rights and pacts with underwater peoples are regulated.

Lakua's prosperity does not only come from the lake. The lands surrounding the Kentron are among the most fertile in all of Oreka. Its fields produce bountiful harvests year after year, and its orchards yield fruits that are especially sweet and prized in distant markets. Lakua's grain feeds entire regions, and its wines and preserves travel by land and lake routes with the stamp of quality that distinguishes the region.

The combination of its agricultural wealth and its mastery of water routes has made it one of the most productive and economically advanced regions in the world. Its shipyards are renowned, its markets are always well supplied and its population enjoys a stability rare in Glastra.
