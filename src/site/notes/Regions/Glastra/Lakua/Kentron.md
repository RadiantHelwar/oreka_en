# Kentron

The largest lake in the world. Or so say the people who make their living from its waters, and no one has been able to prove otherwise.

It occupies more than sixty percent of the [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Lakua\|Lakua]] region, the lake is so extense that many consider that Lakua is not a land with a lake, but a lake dotted with land. Its deep blue, ever-changing waters are full of life and richness. Shoals of migrating fish, giant molluscs and luminous algae support entire economies.

Fishing, merchant and passenger ships criss-cross it constantly, tracing well-known routes between the many islands that emerge within. Some are mere boulders; others are home to port cities, lake monasteries or fortresses built on ancient petrified reefs.

Beneath the surface, Kentron is another world. Societies of Tritons, Merfolk and Aquatic Elves thrive in its depths, organised in underwater kingdoms, inhabited reefs and carved coral cities. Relations with surface peoples oscillate between trade, tense diplomacy and watchful silence.

The lake is said to be bottomless at certain points. And that in those chasms, where the light cannot reach, lie countless secrets, attracting adventurers from all parts of Oreka.
