# Great Tree of Ilios

![Ilios2.png](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Ilios2.png)

> Like an olive tree of light that casts no shadow, Ilios seems eternal and unchanging. 
> But no tree can sustain the world forever if it is required to forget who it was.
>
> _Sazzalazz, Poet of the Green Scales_.

The Great Tree of Ilios appears as a colossal olive tree of golden light, a non-solid projection, almost a divine hologram suspended above the continent of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]].

The tree is of an impossible scale:
its branches extend above the clouds, visible from any point on the continent, while its trunk vanishes before it touches the ground, as if it had never really been anchored to the physical world. The blue sky can be seen through it, and the clouds pass beneath its branches, reinforcing the sense that it does not quite belong to this plane.

Ilios is said to process the light of the sun and convert it into energy, promoting fertility and abundance for all the lands of [[Regions/Oreka\|Oreka]].

Previously, before the [[Lore/Cosmology/The Rot\|The Rot]] Ilios was a solid, tangible, **real** tree.

Its function was the same as that of today's tree, but according to the [[Lore/Pantheons/Church of Aureon\|Church]] the wickedness of the [[Lore/Ancient Past/Four Fiend Kings\|Four Fiend Kings]] invaded Oreka's energy stream in an insidious way, secret even to [[Secretos/Cabal/Aureon\|Aureon]] himself before his ascension.

This corruption seeped down to the roots of Ilios, perverting all those who lived underground next to them and eventually causing the [[Lore/Cosmology/The Rot\|The Rot]].

The world declined during this period: abundance was lost and, while the people of [[Regions/Oreka\|Oreka]] were fighting for the few pieces of land that still bore fruit, the tree became completely diseased.

Then [[Secretos/Cabal/Aureon\|Aureon]] took one of the last direct actions he would take as a deity: he made the diseased Ilios disappear and replaced it with the golden form that exists today, eliminating the problem of the [[Lore/Cosmology/The Rot\|The Rot]].

To this day, the branches of Ilios are seen from coast to coast of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] and its energy promotes abundance throughout [[Regions/Oreka\|Oreka]].
