# Dartza

**Capital city**: Keratu
**Population**: Leonin, Humans, Fauns, Anarenelin Elves.

The Dartza is a border region located in the south of [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]], on the continent of [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]]. Famous for its unrivalled ceramics production, today it is the epicentre of geopolitical tension between the Empire of [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] and the nations of Glastra after being conquered twenty years ago.

## History and Occupation

Historically, the Dartza was an integral part of the [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]] a land proud of its independence. However, after two centuries of intermittent warfare with the Elvish Empire of [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] The region was captured during the last major conflict two decades ago.

Following the fall of the Dartza, the [[Last Truce\|Last Truce]], a peace treaty without an enddate, that maintains the _status quo_: [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]] retains control of the region in exchange for a mutual commitment to proportional disarmament and de-escalation of hostilities with the government of [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]].

## Society and Caste System

Under the rule of the elves *Anarenelin*, the Dartza have been integrated into the Ergo caste system.

- **Social Status:** The original inhabitants of the Dartza are treated as citizens of the lower rungs of the Imperial social pyramid.

- **Quality of Life:** Despite their low status, the quality of life is superior to that of the middle class of Kostakt. Ergo's laws ensure that no one goes hungry, universal access to schooling for children and opportunities for training in technology and magic for adults.

- **Generational divide:** There is a growing gap between the old-timers who remember the independent Dartza and the young people who have grown up under the Ergonian system and know nothing else, appreciating the stability and security it offers.

## Resistance and Conflict

The occupation has spawned two main insurgent movements operating from Kostakt:

1. [[Lore/Organizations/Reconquista\|Reconquista]]: Led by the half-elf ex-commander [[Alhor Stammets\|Alhor Stammets]], seeks the political restoration of the Dartza through traditional military methods, and the replacement or elimination of the royal house of Kostakt, which ceded its lands to the enemy.

2. [[Lore/Organizations/Hunger Brigade\|Hunger Brigade]]: A radical faction led by the Leonin [[Frath Arguul\|Frath Arguul]]. They demand the total annihilation of elves and those of elven descent and resort to demonology and infernal pacts, considering enemies everyone not part of their group, including those citizens of the Dartza who do not join their cause.
