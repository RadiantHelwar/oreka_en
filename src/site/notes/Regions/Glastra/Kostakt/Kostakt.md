# Kostakt

**Capital**: [[Pradka\|Pradka]]
**Inhabitants**: Humans, Halfling, Leonin, Tabaxi, Zaharak, Fauns.

Kostakt was born from diverse communities that valued a placid, self-sufficient life. Millennia before the rise of Aureon, these communities unified under a single identity - Kostakt - when threatened by the expansion of neighbouring territories.

The circumstances of Kostakt's creation made it a land wary of its neighbours. Not hostile, but deeply proud of its independence. Its royal family has ruled ever since, maintaining a dynastic stability rare in the region, and its people have come a long way from being simple farmers and herders, to a society much more heterogeneous and open to the opportunities and diversity of its people.

From their origins as distinct communities, the different sub-regions were formed, each with their own idiosyncrasies. One region had the best wines, another the best artists, one was proud of its military, another of its philosophers...

For 200 years, Kostakt has been intermittently at war with [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]], the dominant country on the continent of [[Regions/Tomoe/Tomoe\|Tomoe]] to the south. The elven Empire of Ergo has been relentlessly trying to invade the lands of Kostakt, until it succeeded 20 years ago, at least in part, by conquering the region of the [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Dartza\|Dartza]], formerly known for its unique ceramics. This was followed by the signing of the [[Last Truce\|Last Truce]], by which the _Status Quo_ was maintained, with the [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Dartza\|Dartza]] in Ergo's hands, and both countries agreed to a military de-escalation.

Shortly afterwards, the rebel group [[Lore/Organizations/Reconquista\|Reconquista]] emerged, whose goal is to restore the Dartza and place it back in the hands of the people of Kostakt. Seven years ago, however, a new group branched from Reconquista, publicly rejected by Reconquista, which insists on the total annihilation of Ergo and all elves, those with elven blood and their allies in the region of Kostakt: the [[Lore/Organizations/Hunger Brigade\|Hunger Brigade]].

The simultaneous existence of Reconquista and the Hunger Brigade has fractured Kostakt's internal debate, dividing its population between those who advocate political restoration, those who advocate pragmatic coexistence with Ergo, and those who clamour for all-out war.
