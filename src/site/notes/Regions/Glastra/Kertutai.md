# Kertutai

**Name:** Kertutai ("Home of the Swarm", misinterpreted as a proper name by other cultures)

**Shape:** Resembling a hollow colossal tree limb suspended between two mountain ranges

**Capital**: Palegia

**Government**: Eusocial Monarchy

**Key geography**: It encompasses the only safe passage over the [[Regions/Glastra/Harana\|Harana]] between [[Regions/Glastra/Haizea\|Haizea]] and [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Lakua\|Lakua]].

# General Description

Kertutai is a unique realm in Oreka, built inside what was once the Great fallen branch of [[Regions/Glastra/Ilios\|Ilios]]. After centuries of work - purification, excavation, engineering and renovation - the Kitraki replaced all the original wood with their own materials: hardened resins, mineral waxes, treated clays and massive chitin structures.

Although nothing remains of the tree limb, the whole country retains the silhouette of the colossal branch, a kind of living bridge linking the mountain ranges of [[Mendile\|Mendile]] (northwest) and [[Lerroa\|Lerroa]] (south-east).

This bridge is the reason why Kertutai is crucial:
- it is the only safe crossing of the Harana.

# History and Mission

According to the Kitraki themselves, their deity Itia rescued them from chaos. According to their stories, they were born out of corruption during the [[Lore/Cosmology/The Rot\|The Rot]] as monsters, until Itia endowed them with reason, leaving behind their monstrous nature.

In return, she only asked them to help contain the corruption and help their neighbours. The Kitraki gratefully vowed to purify the fallen branch and destroy all those who would threaten the newly created region of [[Regions/Glastra/Harana\|Harana]] and its neighbours in [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Lakua\|Lakua]] y [[Regions/Glastra/Haizea\|Haizea]].

Today, Kertutai is a militarily compact, diplomatically tight and economically strategic state whose revenues depend on control of the pass.


## The Four Sections of Kertutai

### 1- The Avenue

The corridor that foreigners are familiar with. It runs along the north side of Kertutai, next to the outer wall, with large open arches that give the impression of being completely in the open air. It is where caravans, travellers and goods pass from one side of the Harana to the other.

The arches can be closed with steel-like membranes, and there are elevated guard posts along the avenue.

There are detours, always guarded, leading to internal areas where foreigners are almost never admitted, and there are always guards ensuring that this rule is enforced.

Fees are paid here, documents are checked and traffic regulations are issued.


### 2- The Hive

The real life of Kertutai.

A vertical labyrinth of hexagonal chambers, curved ramps and living platforms where most of the population lives.

It breathes teamwork, collective droning singing, resinous perfumes and warm filtered light.

For an outsider it would be like entering a living organism.


### 3. The Aerial

The official air base.

Any flight in or out of Kertutai must be registered here.

It is the largest concentration of Kitraki female warriors.

Its flight halls are caverns open to the sky, protected by taut membranes.

From here, patrols set out to guard the Harana and escort convoys.

The hum emanating from the Aerial can be heard for miles on windy days.


### 4. Palegia (The Palace)

The jewel of the kingdom.

A series of inverted towers hanging below the main body of Kertutai, linked by cascading terraces where water collects in great shimmering pools.

From the terraces, water cascades down into the Harana in iridescent liquid columns.

Palegia is home to:

- The Matrix Queen
- The Wise Ones
- The ruling castes

It is a sacred place, where few outsiders have ever set foot.
