# Glastra

Glastra is the heart of Oreka: a fertile, diverse and densely populated continent, where the bounty brought by the Great Tree of Ilios allowed kingdoms, city-states, religions and trade routes to flourish. Here coexist the power of the Church of Aureon, ancient nations in decline, young democracies and still untamed wilderness. It is a land of opportunity, hidden tensions and fragile balances, where almost everyone has something to gain... or to lose.

## Regions


- [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Lakua\|Lakua]] - The central region of Glastra, located in the valley of the same name.
	- [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Riza\|Riza]], the continent's flagship city, political and commercial centre.
	- [[Regions/Glastra/Lakua/Kentron\|Kentron]], the world's largest lake, which occupies a large part of the region.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Ieró\|Ieró]], the capital of the Church of Aureon, with its imposing Palace: [[Sanctum\|Sanctum]].
- [[Regions/Glastra/Haizea\|Haizea]], land of cliffs and high nobility.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Kertutai\|Kertutai]], the hive of the [[Especies/Kitraki\|Kitraki]].
- [[Regions/Glastra/Harana\|Harana]], the forbidden valley.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Tisaran Theocracy\|Tisaran Theocracy]], the country that the Zaharak forged for themselves.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Irrigos\|Irrigos]], a merchant dominion with a powerful naval navy.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Bardhore\|Bardhore]], ancestral home of the dwarves, who have ruled both above and below the mountains for millennia.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Demenor\|Demenor]], known as the Old Empire, now a young democracy rediscovering how to walk before running.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Cínere\|Cínere]], the land of contrasts: huge rivers and green jungles, but also arid deserts and salt flats.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Evisnos\|Evisnos]], the region of hills and dragons. Its inhabitants are mostly Dragonborn, and its governor is [[Ilmathar\|Ilmathar]], an old and benevolent bronze dragon.
- [[Regions/Glastra/The Shires\|The Shires]], a region criss-crossed by rivers, lakes and deltas, divided into zones of different sizes, inhabitants and cultures. United only by a pact and a common will to work together.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Kostakt/Kostakt\|Kostakt]], known as the Southern Frontier, the scene of centuries of intermittent warfare with [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]].
- [[Regions/Glastra/Maraton/Marathon\|Marathon]], land of the festival, famous for its coastline, tourist destinations and... dubious legality.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Eldaren\|Eldaren]], home of the _Eldarenelin_, Elves of the Stars. Wary and jealous of their borders; few have seen their mountainous forests at close quarters and lived to tell the tale.
- [[Regions/Glastra/Ithiren\|Ithiren]], land of the _Ithirenelin_, Elves of the Moons. Most friendly to other races among all elves, their beautiful silver kingdom welcomes visitors, though never as equals.
