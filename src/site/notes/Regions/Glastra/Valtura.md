# Valtura

The capital of Haizea, Valtura, sits on the side of a great ridge called Turhaz. Its lower districts are divided on stone terraces, with sloping roofs to withstand the wind.

At the top, literally clinging to the vertical wall of the ridge, stands the __Castle of the Ragged Clouds__: a fortress whose foundations are anchored in the vertical wall of the _Dawnspire_. Its towers protrude beyond the edge of the cliff, as if trying to catch the clouds. From below, it looks like a miracle of architecture and a challenge to the winds.

Haizean tradition has it that Valtura is the second capital of the kingdom, after the first was swallowed up by the [[Eternal Labyrinth\|Eternal Labyrinth]] in the first centuries of its history.
