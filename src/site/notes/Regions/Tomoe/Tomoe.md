# Tomoe

Tomoe is a continent of lush forests and jungles, vast arable land and beautiful landscapes full of life, as long as we are talking about the western part, where [[Regions/Tomoe/Ergo\|Ergo]], the Empire of the Sun, maintains the abundance with its magic.

The eastern part of the continent is arid and desert-like, home to few creatures and even fewer civilisations.
