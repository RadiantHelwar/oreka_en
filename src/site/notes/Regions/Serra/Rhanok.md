# Rhanok

[[Regions/Serra/Rhanok\|Rhanok]] is the largest island of the fragmented continent of [[Regions/Serra/Serra\|Serra]] and one of the most stable territories in the whole archipelago. Situated at the eastern end of Serra, it marks the beginning of the **Fourth Belt**, one of the great rows of islands born from the tectonic faults that tore the continent apart after the emergence of [[Regions/Glastra/Ilios\|Ilios]].

Unlike many other Serran lands, which were dominated by rival clans, pirates or ephemeral settlements, Rhanok managed to become a relatively unified nation over the centuries. Its capital, [[Regions/Serra/Dom\|Dom]] is one of the oldest and most populated ports in the archipelago and acts as a meeting point for sailors, traders and refugees from countless cultures.

The very name of the island itself comes from the ancient Orc word "Rhanok", meaning *"Reunion"*, reflecting the role that this territory has played since its earliest days: a place where many different peoples found refuge and began a new life.

## Reunion Island

The first communities to settle on Rhanok did so in the years after the great cataclysm that fragmented Serra. At that time, the archipelago was a devastated territory: flooded coastlines, unpredictable seas and still active volcanoes marked the landscape.

In the midst of this chaos, a settlement sprang up on the eastern coast of the island. The small community welcomed anyone who came in search of refuge or a new opportunity. Over time, the place came to be known simply as [[Regions/Serra/Dom\|Dom]] , a word which in the Orc language means _home_.

As survivors of different races arrived on the island, the settlement grew into a stable port city. From there, many of its inhabitants began to explore and colonise other nearby islands, establishing sea lanes and small enclaves scattered throughout the region.

Over the centuries, Rhanok evolved from a simple refuge to become the political and economic centre of much of the Fourth Band.

## Geography

Rhanok is a large and diverse island, shaped by its volcanic origins.

Its eastern coastline is marked by wide natural bays, which favoured the growth of [[Regions/Serra/Dom\|Dom]] and other smaller ports. These waters are relatively calm compared to the more violent seas of the archipelago, making the region an important maritime transit point.

The interior of the island is more rugged. Volcanic ridges, dense forests and fertile valleys form a varied landscape dotted with villages and fortresses. Some volcanoes are still active, though most have been dormant for centuries.

The abundance of fertile volcanic soils made Rhanok one of the few lands in Serra capable of supporting large populations without relying solely on fishing or trade with Glastra and Tomoe.

## Villages of Rhanok

Since its foundation, Rhanok has been a land of cultural mix.

Orcs, humans, dwarves, halflings and other races found refuge on the island after the collapse of the old continent. Many of these communities were used to surviving in harsh conditions, allowing them to adapt quickly to Serra's dangerous environment.

Over the generations, these cultures became deeply intermingled. Ancient racial differences became less important in the face of a common identity: that of the *rhanokers*, people of the sea and the frontier who value self-reliance, hospitality and the strength to defend what they call home.

Even so, the influence of Orc traditions is still visible in many aspects of local culture, from certain language terms to community rituals and warrior customs.

## Rhanok Today

Today, Rhanok acts as one of the main centres of stability in Serra. Although the archipelago remains a dangerous and fragmented region, the influence of Dom and their allies maintains a degree of order in much of the Fourth Band.

The island continues to be a gathering point for peoples of all kinds: sailors crossing dangerous seas, clans seeking alliances, merchants pursuing new trade routes, or exiles needing a fresh start.

On a continent marked by violence and uncertainty, Rhanok continues to represent what its name has meant all along: a place where the people of the world can come together and call a new land home.

## Red Fang

Rhanok is also home to the main headquarters of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Red Fang\|Red Fang]], one of the Four [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Great Guilds\|Great Guilds]] from [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Mercenaries\|Mercenaries]] of Oreka. The organisation maintains its stronghold and command centre in the vicinity of [[Regions/Serra/Dom\|Dom]] from where he coordinates contracts, recruitment and expeditions throughout the Serra and beyond.

Unlike other mercenary guilds, Red Fang has a deeply rooted relationship with the cultures of the archipelago. Traditionally, the guild leader is not only chosen by the mercenaries themselves, but is also recognised as the head of the most powerful clan or tribe on Serra. This custom has created an inseparable bond between the organisation and the people of the archipelago.

As a result, the Red Fang are not seen simply as a group of hired soldiers, but as a Serran institution. Its warriors are considered to represent the strength and indomitable spirit of the islands, and their leadership often plays an important role in the political balance between clans, settlements and cities in the region.

Although the Guild's mercenaries accept contracts around the world, their ultimate allegiance remains tied to Rhanok and the communities that grew up around [[Regions/Serra/Dom\|Dom]], where the organisation maintains its roots.
