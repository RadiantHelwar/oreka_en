# Dom, the Home City

Dom is the heart of Rhanok and the most important town on the island.

Built around a large natural harbour, the city grew over time to become a centre of trade, navigation and diplomacy between the many islands of Serra. Its docks receive ships from all over the archipelago, as well as from other continents of [[Regions/Oreka\|Oreka]].

The city is known for its open character. From its origins, Dom has been defined by a simple but powerful idea: anyone who arrives in peace can find a place to belong.

This principle has attracted generations of refugees, mercenaries, traders and explorers, making the city one of the most diverse places in the known world.

It is home to the headquarters of [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Red Fang\|Red Fang]].
