# Pagos Glacier

! [pagos.jpg](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Zonas/pagos.jpg)

- Region: [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]], [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]
- Neighbouring Regions: Kingdom of [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]], [[Faro Skia\|Faro Skia]]

The Pagos Glacier is a vast glacial formation eroded by constant winds that, over the centuries, have carved deep corridors open to the sky. The upper surface is impassable due to the presence of sharp ridges and ice spines, so transit is only possible through the narrow passages below.

These corridors channel the wind with great violence, reducing visibility and making progress difficult. As a result, the glacier is known as a dangerous pass even for experienced travellers, and as a natural refuge for creatures adapted to cold and isolation.

On the other side of the glacier lies the inaccessible [[Faro Skia\|Faro Skia]].
