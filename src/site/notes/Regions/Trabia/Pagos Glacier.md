# Pagos Glacier

![pagos.jpg](/img/user/Extras/Imagenes/Zonas/pagos.jpg)

- Region: [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]], [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]
- Neighbouring Regions: Kingdom of [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]], [[Skia Lighthouse\|Skia Lighthouse]]

The Pagos Glacier is a vast glacial formation sculpted by relentless winds that, over the centuries, have carved deep corridors open to the sky. The upper surface is impassable due to sharp ridges and ice spines, so transit is only possible through the narrow passages below.

These corridors channel the wind with great force, reducing visibility and making progress arduous. As a result, the glacier is known as a perilous passage even for experienced travellers, and as a natural refuge for creatures adapted to cold and isolation.

Beyond the glacier lies the inaccessible [[Skia Lighthouse\|Skia Lighthouse]].
