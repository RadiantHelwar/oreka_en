# Adamas Capital

Type: City
Region: [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]], [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]
Ethnic origin: Hirubense
Important buildings: Palacio Real Eguzkion, Sede de Cierzo, El Ensanche Arcano.

Adamas Capital, or _Hiruburu_ to historians, is the heart of the Kingdom of Adamas. Situated far from the ice of the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]] The city combines the majesty of towers and walls with the vitality of markets, guilds and magic academies. To be a magician here is not a pastime, but a respected profession: Hirubans use magic to keep warm during the long winters and to encourage harvests in a land increasingly reluctant to bear fruit. The city reflects both the wealth and military might of the kingdom, but also its arcane tradition: sorcerers, enchanters and druids coexist alongside craftsmen and merchants, making Adamas a centre where politics, trade and magic intertwine without losing the sense of a safe and prosperous bastion.

At the heart of the city stands the **Eguzkion Royal Palace**, residence of the Suntsiezina and symbol of the kingdom's power. Near the central square stands the guild house [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Northwind\|Northwind]] The **Ensanche Arcano**, a tall and dazzling building that, while more practical than a palace, does not lose its imposing presence. Towards one of the newer quarters, the **Ensanche Arcano** brings together schools of magic, component shops and research centres, making it a hotbed of knowledge and sorcery that defines much of Hiruban life.
