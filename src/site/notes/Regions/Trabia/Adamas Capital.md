# Adamas Capital

**Type**: City
**Region**: [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas\|Adamas]], [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]
**Demonym**: Hiruban
**Important buildings**: Eguzkion Royal Palace, Northwind Headquarters, Arcane Annex.

Adamas Capital, or _Hiruburu_ to historians, is the heart of the Kingdom of Adamas. Situated far from the ice of the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]], the city combines the majesty of towers and walls with the vitality of markets, guilds, and magical academies. To be a magician here is not merely a pastime, but a respected profession: Hirubans employ magic to keep warm during the long winters and to encourage harvests in a land increasingly reluctant to bear fruit. The city reflects both the wealth and military might of the kingdom, but also its arcane tradition: wizards, sorcerers, and druids coexist alongside craftsmen and merchants, making Adamas a centre where politics, trade, and magic intertwine, without ever losing the sense of a safe and prosperous bastion.

At the heart of the city stands the **Eguzkion Royal Palace**, residence of the Suntsiezina and symbol of the kingdom's power. Near the central square is the [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Northwind\|Northwind]] Headquarters, a tall and dazzling building that, while more practical than a palace, does not lack imposing presence. In one of the newer quarters, the **Arcane Annex** brings together schools of magic, component shops, and research centres, creating a hub of knowledge and sorcery that defines much of Hiruban life.
