# Kingdom of Adamas

**Type**: Kingdom
**Location**: North of [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]
**Adjacent to [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]
**Capital**: [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas Capital\|Adamas Capital]]
**Population**: Humans, Goliath, Eldanelelin Elves, Dragonborn, all others.

The Kingdom of Adamas stretches across the north of [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]] in the Arctic regions near the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]. It is an ancient kingdom, which has existed in one form or another since before the dawn of time. [[Calendario Áureo\|Calendario Áureo]] and has maintained its independence and relevance throughout the Eras.

Adamas has been since the beginning of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]] a bulwark against the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Inmortal Army\|Inmortal Army]] from [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]] preventing their forces from crossing the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]. This role has defined much of its history and strategic importance on the continent in recent centuries.

The kingdom has been ruled by the royal house **Suntsiezina** for thousands of years. Its members are said to have elven blood, having lived for centuries, though they are human in appearance: tall and distinguished in bearing. The current king, **Indartsua Suntsiezina IX**, is known for both his combat prowess and his mastery of magic.

The population of Adamas is diverse: humans, goliath, elves and dragonborn make up the majority, though other races also live in smaller numbers. One of the Four Great Guilds of Oreka, [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Northwind\|Northwind]] is based in Adamas and holds a contract with the royal house to help defend the Pagos Glacier. The king himself sits on the guild's council.
