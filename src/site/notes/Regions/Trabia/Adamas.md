# Kingdom of Adamas

**Type**: Kingdom
**Location**: North of [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]]
**Adjacent to**: [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]
**Capital**: [[Regions/Trabia/Adamas Capital\|Adamas Capital]]
**Population**: Humans, Goliath, Ithirenelin Elves, Dragonborn, all others.

The **Kingdom of Adamas** stretches across the northern reaches of [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]], in the arctic regions bordering the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]. It is an ancient realm that has existed in one form or another since before the dawn of the [[Golden Calendar\|Golden Calendar]], maintaining both its independence and its relevance throughout the Eras.

Since the beginning of the [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Wave of Darkness\|Wave of Darkness]], Adamas has served as a bulwark against the [[Immortal Army\|Immortal Army]] of [[Lore/Wave of Darkness/Skia, the Princess of Shadows\|Skia, the Princess of Shadows]], preventing their forces from crossing the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]. This role has shaped much of the kingdom’s history and explains its considerable strategic importance on the continent in recent centuries.

The realm has been ruled by the royal house **Suntsiezina** for thousands of years. Its members are said to possess elven blood, for they live for centuries despite appearing fully human—tall, pale, and distinguished in bearing. The current king, **Indartsua Suntsiezina IX**, is renowned both for his prowess in battle and for his mastery of magic.

The population of Adamas is notably diverse. Humans, goliaths, elves, and dragonborn form the majority, though many other peoples live within the kingdom in smaller numbers. One of the Four Great Guilds of Oreka, [[Lore/Organizations/Guilds/Northwind\|Northwind]], is based in Adamas and holds a contract with the royal house to assist in the defence of the [[Regions/Trabia/Pagos Glacier\|Pagos Glacier]]. The king himself sits on the guild’s council.
