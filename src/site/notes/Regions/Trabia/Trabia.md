# Trabia

Trabia is the largest continent, though scarcity and drought are plaguing it. It is harsh and sparsely populated, dominated by extreme climates, vast frozen expanses and regions where survival is never guaranteed. Its people have learned to rely on each other to resist scarcity and isolation, developing tough, distrustful and tightly knit societies. Threats from the north and echoes of ancient wars continue to mark its identity, making Trabia a constant frontier between resistance and collapse.
