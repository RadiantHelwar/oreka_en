# Trabia

Trabia is the largest continent, though it is plagued by scarcity and drought. It is harsh and sparsely populated, dominated by extreme climates: vast frozen expanses in the far north, wide arid regions in the south, and temperate or challenging lands in between where survival is never guaranteed. Its people have learned to rely on one another to endure scarcity and isolation, developing tough, wary, and tightly knit societies. Threats from the north and echoes of ancient wars continue to shape its identity, making Trabia a constant frontier between resilience and collapse.
