# Lehar

## The Green Moon

It is Oreka's second moon in size, although it may be the larger of the two as when the two cross each other in the sky, Lehar is always behind [[Regions/The Moons/Damar\|Damar]]. This means that it is further apart and this makes it difficult to know which one is really bigger.

It has a distinct greenish glow, and atmospheric phenomena can be distinguished on its surface. There also appear to be masses of water, making it an unusually active celestial body.

Lehar is in synchronous rotation with Oreka. Only one side of Lehar can be seen from the world; the other side remains eternally hidden.

Its full cycle lasts 56 days.
