## The Silver Moon

The largest of Oreka's visible moons, or at least the one that appears so as it dominates the sky. Pale and silvery in hue, its surface shows dark valleys and vast light plains that can be distinguished even with the naked eye.

Unlike [[Regions/The Moons/Lehar\|Lehar]], Damar shows no visible signs of atmospheric activity. Her face remains immutable, almost stern, as if contemplating the world from an eternal stillness.

Its full cycle lasts 28 days, marking the natural rhythm of the tides and of many ancient calendars.
