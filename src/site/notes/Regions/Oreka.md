# Oreka

Oreka means balance. This name was given to the world by the ancient saviours, of which it was a part. [[Lore/Pantheons/Aureon (God)\|Aureon]] before his ascension.

The world of Oreka is divided into four continents:

- [[Regions/Glastra/Glastra\|Glastra]] in the centre, home of the **Great Tree of [[Regions/Glastra/Ilios\|Ilios]] **.
- [[Regions/Trabia/Trabia\|Trabia]] to the northeast, the largest continent of all.
- [[Regions/Tomoe/Tomoe\|Tomoe]] to the south-southeast, the lung of Oreka.
- [[Regions/Serra/Rhanok\|Rhanok]] to the west, the archipelago.
